# NGO & Non-Profits — Charities / Foundations

> Focus: Credibility & Impact

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Smile Foundation | https://www.smilefoundationindia.org/ | Emotional hero imagery, persistent "Donate Now" CTA, impact counter-up, programme-centric navigation with branded names |
| Akshaya Patra | https://www.akshayapatra.org/ | Single powerful stat hero ("2.35M meals daily"), transparency/accountability section, international donation routing |
| Goonj | https://goonj.org/ | Material donation model (clothing/goods), collection camp locator, campaign progress bars, volunteer emphasis |
| CRY | https://www.cry.org/ | Multiple donation pathways (UPI, cards, monthly, legacy), tax exemption messaging near donate buttons |
| Raise India Foundation | https://www.raiseindiafoundation.org/ | Hospital partnership branding, seasonal campaigns, government registration details for legitimacy |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Emotional beneficiary imagery throughout with empowering headlines
- Persistent "Donate Now" floating button or header CTA
- Impact numbers prominently displayed with counter-up animation
- Tax benefit messaging (80G) near donation CTAs
- Programme cards grid with branded initiative names
- Beneficiary success stories carousel
- Transparency section (audit compliance, annual reports, certifications)
- Multiple donation pathways and recurring donation emphasis
- Volunteer/Get Involved section alongside monetary donations
- Newsletter signup for ongoing engagement

## Variant A — Education-Focused NGO / Children's Charity

**Page 1: Home**

1. **titlebar**
   NGO name/logo, nav (Our Work, Programs, Impact, Get Involved, Donate), button ("Donate Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Impactful image (children learning/playing), heading ("Every Child Deserves a Chance to Learn") + paragraph (mission statement) + two buttons ("Donate Now", "See Our Impact").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **counter-up (Impact Numbers)**
   "10,000+ Children Educated", "50+ Schools Supported", "15+ Years of Impact", "95% of Funds Reach Beneficiaries".

5. **columnsgrid (Our Programs — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + paragraph + button. School Infrastructure, Teacher Training, Scholarship Program, Digital Learning, Mid-Day Meals, Girl Child Education.

6. **layout-row (Our Story)**
   image (founder/team) + heading + paragraph (origin story, vision, mission) + badge ("Registered Non-Profit").

7. **tabs (Impact Stories)**
   2-3 featured stories: image + heading + paragraph (individual transformation story) + button ("Read More").

8. **progress-bar (Current Campaign Goals)**
   Multiple bars: "Build 5 New Classrooms — 72% Funded", "1000 Scholarships — 60% Achieved", "Digital Lab Setup — 45% Complete".

9. **layout-row (How You Can Help)**
   columnsgrid (3 cols): icon + heading + paragraph + button. Donate, Volunteer, Corporate Partnership.

10. **layout-row (Donate Section)**
   heading + paragraph + columnsgrid of donation amounts: "₹500 — Feeds a child for a month", "₹2000 — School supplies for a year", "₹10,000 — Sponsor a child's education" + button each.

11. **ticker (Partners & Supporters)**
    Scrolling corporate partner and donor logos.

12. **carousel (Testimonials from Beneficiaries & Donors)**
    blockquote + image + caption.

13. **layout-row (Transparency)**
    heading + paragraph (annual reports, audited accounts) + button ("View Annual Report") + badges (FCRA, 80G, 12A registrations).

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, email, FCRA number, 80G info, social media, newsletter signup.

**Page 2: Impact Report**

1. **counter-up** (yearly stats)
2. **columnsgrid** (project-wise progress with progress-bars)
3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **accordion** (financial summary)

## Variant B — Health / Humanitarian NGO

**Page 1: Home**

1. **titlebar**
   NGO name, nav (Mission, Programs, Impact, News, Volunteer, Donate), button ("Support Our Cause").

2. **video-background (Hero)**
   Field footage (healthcare camps, relief work), overlay: heading ("Healthcare for the Underserved") + paragraph + two buttons ("Donate", "Learn More").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **counter-up (Impact)**
   "100,000+ Patients Treated", "500+ Health Camps", "20+ Districts Covered", "50+ Partner Hospitals".

5. **columnsgrid (Programs — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + paragraph. Mobile Health Clinics, Maternal & Child Health, Clean Water & Sanitation, Disease Prevention, Mental Health, Emergency Relief.

6. **layout-row (Current Crisis / Campaign)**
   Urgent appeal: image + heading + paragraph + countdown ("Campaign Ends In:") + progress-bar ("₹15L of ₹20L raised") + button ("Donate Now").

7. **tabs (Get Involved)**
   Tabs: Donate, Volunteer, Corporate CSR, Fundraise. Each: paragraph + form or button.

8. **layout-row (Stories of Change)**
   carousel: image + heading + paragraph (beneficiary stories).

9. **layout-row (Our Team)**
   columnsgrid: image + heading + caption (role). Founder, Medical Director, Program Leads.

10. **ticker (Awards & Recognitions)**
   Scrolling award badges and recognition logos.

11. **accordion (FAQs)**
    How donations are used, tax benefits, volunteering requirements, transparency.

12. **layout-row (Transparency & Governance)**
    heading + paragraph + buttons ("Annual Report", "Financial Statements", "Board Members").

13. **form (Get in Touch)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (I want to: Donate / Volunteer / Partner / Media Enquiry), textarea (message), button.

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, email, registration numbers (FCRA, 80G, CSR-1), social, newsletter.

## Variant C — Environmental / Animal Welfare NGO

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   NGO name, nav (About, Campaigns, Adopt, Volunteer, Donate), button ("Take Action").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Beautiful nature/animal image, heading ("Protecting Our Planet, One Act at a Time") + paragraph + two buttons ("Join the Movement", "Donate").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **counter-up (Impact)**
   "5,000+ Trees Planted", "1,000+ Animals Rescued", "50+ Beach Cleanups", "10,000+ Volunteers".

5. **columnsgrid (Our Campaigns — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + paragraph + button. Tree Planting, Beach/River Cleanup, Animal Rescue & Shelter, Wildlife Conservation, Plastic-Free Initiative, Environmental Education.

6. **layout-row (Adopt an Animal)**
   carousel of animals available: image + heading (name) + caption (species, age) + paragraph (story) + button ("Adopt / Sponsor").

7. **layout-row (Upcoming Events)**
   columnsgrid of events: image + heading + calendar date + paragraph + button ("Register"). Beach cleanup drives, tree planting days, awareness workshops.

8. **progress-bar (Campaign Progress)**
   "10,000 Trees Goal — 72% Done", "Plastic-Free 50 Shops — 45%", "Animal Shelter Expansion — 30%".

9. **tabs (Get Involved)**
   Tabs: Volunteer, Donate, Corporate Partnership, Start a Fundraiser. Each: paragraph + form/button.

10. **carousel (Volunteer Stories)**
   Image + blockquote + caption.

11. **ticker (Media Coverage)**
    Scrolling media logos and article headlines.

12. **form (Join Us)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (interest: Volunteer / Donate / Partner / Adopt), textarea (message), button.

13. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, email, registration numbers, social media (all platforms), newsletter signup, "Zero administrative waste" badge.

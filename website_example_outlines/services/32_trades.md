# Skilled Trades — Welding / Carpentry / Masonry / Fencing

> Focus: Craftsmanship & Reliability

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Iron Workers Local | ironworkers.org | Bold industrial palette, project gallery, safety emphasis |
| CustomMade | custommade.com | Artisan marketplace, project showcases, craft videos |
| Fence Factory | fencefactoryrental.com | Product catalog grid, free estimate CTA, area coverage |
| Ana White | ana-white.com | DIY woodworking plans, project gallery, step-by-step |
| ProFence | profencesupply.com | Material comparison tabs, installation gallery, quote form |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Project gallery with before/after shots
- Material comparison tables or tabs
- Service area coverage maps
- Free estimate forms with project type dropdowns
- Certifications and safety compliance badges
- Video showcases of craftsmanship

## Variant A — Welding & Fabrication Shop

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Shop name/logo, nav (Services, Gallery, Materials, About, Contact), badge ("Certified Welders"), button ("Request Quote").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: eyebrow ("Precision Metal Work") + heading ("Custom Welding & Steel Fabrication") + paragraph (structural, ornamental, industrial — certified to AWS standards) + two buttons ("View Our Work", "Get a Quote"). Right: image (welder at work / finished fabrication).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **counter-up (Shop Stats)**
   "2,000+ Projects", "15 Years Experience", "AWS Certified", "24-Hour Turnaround Available".

5. **columnsgrid (Services — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph. Structural Steel, Ornamental Iron, Pipe Welding, Aluminum Fabrication, Repair & Maintenance, Custom Metalwork.

6. **tabs (Project Gallery)**
   Tabs: Structural, Ornamental, Industrial, Custom. Each: columnsgrid of project images + captions.

7. **layout-row (Materials We Work With)**
   columnsgrid (4 cols): icon + heading + caption. Steel, Aluminum, Stainless Steel, Cast Iron.

8. **layout-row (Our Process)**
   4-step: icon + heading + caption. "Consultation & Design" → "Material Sourcing" → "Fabrication & Welding" → "Finishing & Delivery".

9. **carousel (Client Testimonials)**
   blockquote + caption (client, project type).

10. **ticker (Certifications)**
    Scrolling: AWS, ASME, local trade license logos.

11. **form (Request a Quote)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (service type), dropdown (material), textarea (project description), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Phone, email, shop address, hours, certifications, service area.

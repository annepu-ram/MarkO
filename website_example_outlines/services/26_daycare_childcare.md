# Daycare & Childcare — Creches / Play Schools

> Focus: Safety & Trust

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| EuroKids | https://www.eurokidsindia.com/ | Admission-focused hero with age calculator, Harvard-inspired "Heureka" curriculum branding, centre locator, franchise CTA |
| Bachpan Play School | https://www.bachpanglobal.com/ | NEP 2020 compliance branding, Smart Classes technology, Speak-O-Pen nanotech tool, franchise-heavy design |
| KLAY Preschool | https://klay.co.in/ | Age-based programs (months, not years), safety-first (CCTV), daycare + preschool dual offering, after-school care |
| Footprints Childcare | https://www.footprintseducation.in/ | IIT/IIM founder credentials, ParentConnect App, live CCTV access, HighScope US curriculum |
| Little Millennium | https://www.littlemillennium.com/ | Creative program naming, virtual tour feature, 4-step admission process, ROI-focused franchise |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Admission-focused hero with enrollment CTA and age calculator
- Age-based program segmentation (months for daycare, years for preschool)
- Curriculum branding (named approaches: Heureka, HighScope, Montessori)
- Centre locator with city search for nearest branch
- Safety features section (CCTV, child-safe design, parent apps)
- Parent testimonials carousel
- Franchise opportunity section with investment details
- Statistics bar (centers, cities, children enrolled)
- Bright, playful design with rounded corners and primary colors
- Technology features (smart classes, parent apps, virtual tours)

## Variant A — Daycare / Creche

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Center name/logo, nav (Programs, Safety, Gallery, Admissions, Contact), button ("Schedule a Visit").

2. **layout-row (Hero Split)**
   Left: heading ("A Safe, Loving Second Home for Your Little One") + paragraph (nurturing environment, qualified caregivers) + button ("Schedule a Visit"). Right: image (happy children at play).

3. **br (wave divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **counter-up (Trust Indicators)**
   "500+ Families Trust Us", "1:5 Caregiver Ratio", "CCTV Monitored", "10+ Years".

5. **columnsgrid (Our Programs — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph. Infant Care (6m-1yr), Toddler Program (1-2yr), Pre-Nursery (2-3yr), After-School Care, Holiday Camp, Weekend Care.

6. **layout-row (A Day at Our Center)**
   accordion or tabs by time: 8AM Drop-off & Free Play → 9AM Circle Time → 10AM Snack → 10:30 Learning Activity → 12PM Lunch → 1PM Nap Time → 3PM Outdoor Play → 5PM Pickup.

7. **tabs (Safety & Hygiene)**
   Tabs: Physical Safety, Health Protocols, Staff Screening, Emergency Procedures. Each: paragraph + icon grid.

8. **layout-row (Our Caregivers)**
   columnsgrid: image + heading (name) + caption (qualification, experience) + badge ("CPR Certified").

9. **carousel (Our Space)**
   Photos: playrooms, sleep area, outdoor area, kitchen, CCTV setup.

10. **layout-row (Parent Communication)**
   heading + paragraph (daily updates, app notifications, CCTV access, monthly reports) + icon list.

11. **carousel (Parent Testimonials)**
    blockquote + rating + caption (parent name, child's age).

12. **accordion (FAQs)**
    Admission process, fees, timings, meal policy, sick child policy, trial period, CCTV access.

13. **form (Admission Enquiry / Visit)**
    textbox (parent name), textbox (phone), textbox (child's name), textbox (child's age), dropdown (program interest), calendar (visit date), textarea (special needs/requirements), button.

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, email, hours, license number, social media.

## Variant B — Play School / Preschool

1. **titlebar**
   School name/logo, nav (Curriculum, Activities, Admissions, Gallery, Contact), button ("Admissions Open").

2. **carousel (Hero — Happy Learning Moments)**
   Children in activities (art, play, learning) with overlay: heading ("Where Learning Begins with Joy") + button ("Enroll Now").

3. **br (wave divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **ticker (Activities Scroll)**
   Scrolling: icon + caption — Storytelling, Art & Craft, Music, Dance, Outdoor Play, Phonics, Numbers, Sensory Play.

5. **tabs (Our Curriculum)**
   Tabs: Playgroup (2-3yr), Nursery (3-4yr), Jr. KG (4-5yr), Sr. KG (5-6yr). Each: paragraph (learning goals, activities) + image.

6. **columnsgrid (Learning Areas — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + caption. Language Development, Math Readiness, Creative Arts, Physical Development, Social Skills, Science Exploration, Music & Movement, Digital Literacy.

7. **layout-row (Our Approach)**
   heading + paragraph (play-based, Montessori-inspired, child-centered) + image + badge ("CBSE Affiliated" or methodology badge).

8. **layout-row (Our Team)**
   columnsgrid: image + heading + caption (qualification). Principal, teachers, support staff.

9. **carousel (Campus Gallery)**
   Classrooms, playground, activity rooms, garden.

10. **counter-up (School Pride)**
   "1,000+ Alumni", "15+ Years", "1:10 Teacher Ratio", "5-Star Parent Rating".

11. **layout-row (Admissions Process)**
    4-step: icon + heading + caption. "Enquire" → "Campus Visit" → "Registration" → "Welcome Kit".

12. **carousel (Parent Testimonials)**
    blockquote + rating + caption.

13. **form (Admission Enquiry)**
    textbox (parent name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), textbox (child name & age), dropdown (class applying for), calendar (preferred admission date), button.

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, email, hours, affiliation/recognition, social.

## Variant C — After-School / Activity Center for Kids

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Center name, nav (Programs, Schedule, About, Reviews, Contact), button ("Enroll").

2. **layout-row (Hero Split)**
   Left: heading ("Unlock Your Child's Potential After School") + paragraph + button ("Explore Programs"). Right: image (children in activities).

3. **br (wave divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **columnsgrid (Programs — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + caption. Art & Drawing, Robotics & Coding, Dance, Music, Public Speaking, Chess, Creative Writing, Science Club.

5. **tabs (Programs Detail)**
   Tab per program: image + paragraph (what they'll learn, age group, schedule) + counter-up (fee/month) + button.

6. **layout-row (Why After-School Matters)**
   heading + paragraph (skill development, screen-time alternative, social skills, fun) + columnsgrid of benefits: icon + caption.

7. **layout-row (Our Instructors)**
   columnsgrid: image + heading + caption (expertise, certifications).

8. **layout-row (Weekly Schedule)**
   tabs by day: time + program + age group + instructor.

9. **counter-up (Center Stats)**
   "500+ Kids Enrolled", "10+ Programs", "Expert Instructors", "Flexible Schedules".

10. **carousel (Student Work / Achievements)**
   Art, projects, performance photos.

11. **carousel (Parent Reviews)**
    rating + blockquote + caption.

12. **layout-row (Pricing)**
    columnsgrid: Single Program / 2 Programs / All-Access Pass. Each: heading + counter-up + paragraph + button.

13. **form (Enrollment / Trial)**
    textbox (child name), textbox (parent name), textbox (phone), dropdown (program interest), dropdown (age group), button ("Book Free Trial").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, hours (after school timings), social, pickup/drop service info.

# Cleaning Services — Deep Clean / Housekeeping

> Focus: Trust & Convenience

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Safaiwale | https://safaiwale.in/ | 30+ cleaning services catalog, BHK-based pricing, before/after gallery, phone number prominent, blog-heavy SEO |
| Busy Bucket | https://busybucket.in/ | 7-step professional process, trust & safety section (background verification), mechanized cleaning emphasis |
| Urban Company | https://www.urbancompany.com/ | Search-first homepage, multi-category marketplace, standardized pricing, app-first approach, quality promise badges |
| Snabbit | https://snabbit.com/ | "10 Minutes" speed promise as brand identity, app-only model, trust verification heavy, on-demand model |
| AYS Max | https://aysmax.com/ | WhatsApp as primary booking, detailed "what's included" lists, BHK-based pricing, multiple booking channels |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Phone number and WhatsApp prominently displayed for direct booking
- BHK-based pricing (1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK) is India-specific and intuitive
- Before/after galleries provide visual proof of cleaning quality
- Trust & verification section addresses concern about strangers in homes
- 3-step "How It Works" (Book > Clean > Relax) simplicity
- Service breakdown with detailed inclusions removes ambiguity
- Multiple booking channels (phone, WhatsApp, online form, app)
- Review/testimonial focus for trust-critical services

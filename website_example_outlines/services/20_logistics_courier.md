# Logistics & Courier — Local Delivery / Packers & Movers

> Focus: Reliability & Speed

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Agarwal Packers & Movers | https://www.agarwalpackers.com/ | Quote form as PRIMARY hero CTA, consignment tracking feature, step-by-step process timeline, 38+ years trust, insurance emphasis |
| Porter | https://porter.in/ | Uber-like pickup/drop booking interface, vehicle visual comparison, transparent pricing, dual audience (individual + enterprise) |
| Cloud Packers | https://www.cloudpackers.com/ | 30 days free storage, live tracking, pet relocation niche, 24/7 WhatsApp/phone support, discount promotions |
| TheTransporter | https://www.thetransporter.in/ | Eco-friendly packing differentiator, 12+ distinct services, consignment tracking as top-level nav, technology/equipment relocation |
| Rehousing Packers | https://www.rehousingpackers.in/ | Price estimate tool for transparency, promotional discounts (15% off), city-specific landing pages for SEO |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Quote form is THE hero element (instant quote request dominates above-fold)
- Step-by-step process visualization reduces anxiety about moving
- Consignment tracking builds trust and reduces support calls
- Stats/counter-up (years, successful moves, cities covered)
- City-specific pages for local SEO strategy
- Vehicle/service comparison with visual truck size guide
- Discount/promotional banners (price-sensitive market)
- Insurance and safety messaging about careful handling
- WhatsApp as primary booking/support channel
- Pet relocation and specialized services as differentiators

   3-step: icon + heading + paragraph. "Book Online or Call" → "We Pick Up" → "Delivered & Confirmed".

   Image (service area map) + heading + paragraph (areas/zones served) + button ("Check Your Area").

# Food & Beverage — Restaurants / Home Bakers

> Focus: Convenience

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Indian Accent | indianaccent.com | Minimal elegant design, chef spotlight, awards, full-screen hero |
| Bombay Canteen | thebombaycanteen.com | Storytelling-first, Instagram integration, private dining |
| Dhamaka NYC | dhamaka.nyc | Dramatic food photography, reservation as single CTA, press quotes |
| HomeBakers | homebakers.co.in | Search-first hero, city-based filtering, baker profiles with ratings |
| CakeNest | cakenest.in | Occasion-based categories, price-range filtering, same-day delivery |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Reservation is the single most prominent CTA for restaurants
- Photography dominates over text for premium dining
- Chef spotlight positions food as art
- City-based filtering critical for delivery businesses
- Occasion-based categories for cake/gifting
- "How it Works" 3-step for platforms

## Variant A — Restaurant / Dine-In Establishment

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Restaurant logo, nav (Menu, About, Reservations, Gallery, Contact), button ("Reserve a Table").

2. **video-background (Hero)**
   Ambient restaurant footage (cooking, plating, ambiance), overlay: heading ("Flavors That Tell a Story") + paragraph + button ("View Menu").

3. **br (elegant wave divider)**

4. **tabs (Menu Sections)**
   Tabs: Starters, Mains, Desserts, Beverages, Chef's Specials. Each tab: columnsgrid of items — image + heading (dish name) + caption (price) + paragraph (description) + badge ("Chef's Pick" on select items).

5. **layout-row (Our Story)**
   Left: image (chef or restaurant interior). Right: eyebrow ("Since 2010") + heading + paragraph (origin story, philosophy) + badge ("Family-Owned").

6. **carousel (Gallery — Food & Ambiance)**
   High-quality food and interior photos.

7. **counter-up (Numbers)**
   "12+ Years", "50,000+ Happy Diners", "200+ Dishes", "4.7 Google Rating".

8. **ticker (Customer Reviews)**
   Scrolling review cards: rating + blockquote + caption.

9. **layout-row (Reservation CTA)**
   heading + paragraph + form: textbox (name), textbox (phone), dropdown (party size), calendar (date), dropdown (time slot), button ("Reserve Now").

10. **columnsgrid (Private Dining / Events)**
    2-3 cards: image + heading + paragraph. Birthday Parties, Corporate Dinners, Private Events + button ("Enquire").

11. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, hours, phone, social links, Zomato/Swiggy badges.

## Variant B — Home Baker / Cake Artist

**Page: Home (Single Page — Warm & Personal)**

1. **titlebar**
   Baker name/brand, nav (Cakes, Cupcakes, Custom Orders, About Me, Order), icon (cake).

2. **layout-row (Hero Split)**
   Left: heading ("Handcrafted with Love") + paragraph (home baker story, small-batch quality) + button ("Order a Cake"). Right: image (signature cake).

3. **columnsgrid (Signature Items — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + caption (price range) + badge ("Bestseller" on top item).

4. **tabs (Menu Categories)**
   Tabs: Birthday Cakes, Wedding Cakes, Cupcakes, Brownies & Treats, Seasonal Specials. Each: image gallery grid.

5. **layout-row (Custom Cake Section)**
   image (custom cake examples collage) + heading + paragraph (how custom orders work) + button ("Request Custom Cake").

6. **carousel (Customer Celebrations)**
   Customer photos with their cakes at events — rating + caption.

7. **accordion (Ordering Info)**
   Items: Minimum order notice (48 hrs), Delivery areas, Flavors available, Eggless options, Allergen info, Payment methods.

8. **counter-up (Happy Baking Stats)**
   "2,000+ Cakes Delivered", "500+ Custom Designs", "100% Fresh Ingredients", "4.9 Rating".

9. **form (Order Enquiry)**
   textbox (name), textbox (phone/WhatsApp), dropdown (cake type), dropdown (size/servings), calendar (delivery date), textarea (design notes), button ("Send Enquiry").

10. **layout-row (Footer)**
    WhatsApp link (prominent), Instagram, delivery areas, FSSAI info.

## Variant C — Cloud Kitchen / Delivery-Only Restaurant

**Page: Home (Conversion-Focused)**

1. **titlebar**
   Brand logo, nav (Menu, How It Works, Areas, Reviews), badge ("Order Online"), button ("Order Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width food image, overlay: heading ("Restaurant-Quality Meals at Your Doorstep") + paragraph + two buttons ("View Menu", "Order on Swiggy/Zomato").

3. **ticker (Delivery Platforms)**
   Scrolling platform logos: Swiggy, Zomato, direct ordering.

4. **layout-row (How It Works — 3 Steps)**
   columnsgrid: icon + heading + paragraph. "Choose Your Meal" → "We Cook Fresh" → "Delivered Hot".

5. **tabs (Today's Menu)**
   Tabs: Lunch Combos, Dinner Specials, Biryanis, Rolls & Wraps, Desserts. Each: item cards with image + name + price + button.

6. **columnsgrid (Meal Combos — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + paragraph (what's included) + counter-up (price) + badge ("Value Pack") + button.

7. **counter-up (Delivery Stats)**
   "10,000+ Orders Delivered", "30 Min Average Delivery", "4.5+ Rating", "50+ Menu Items".

8. **carousel (Customer Reviews)**
   Rating + blockquote + caption (from delivery platforms).

9. **layout-row (Delivery Areas)**
   heading + columnsgrid of area names/badges + paragraph ("Don't see your area? Contact us!").

10. **form (Catering / Bulk Orders)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), dropdown (occasion), textbox (guest count), calendar (date), button.

11. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Ordering links, contact, delivery hours, FSSAI badge, social links.

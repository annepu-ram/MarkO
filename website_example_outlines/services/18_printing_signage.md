# Printing & Signage — Print Shops / Trophy Makers

> Focus: Custom Orders

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| PrintStop | https://www.printstop.co.in | Product category grid as primary conversion, "Design Now" CTA per product, sample pack for tactile concern, quality guarantee badges |
| FlexiPrint | https://www.flexiprint.in | Personal point of contact differentiator, sample pack prominent, custom quote form, production team review emphasis |
| Thomson Press | https://www.thomsonpress.com | Industrial/corporate B2B, multi-factory infrastructure, legacy since 1967, product-oriented, sales offices for meetings |
| Galaxy Signage | https://www.galaxysignage.com | Portfolio/gallery as primary proof, industry-vertical targeting, CNC/technology emphasis, client logo bar for B2B trust |
| Cosign India | https://cosign.in | Premium/architectural positioning, modular system approach, global presence, application-based gallery (context, not just product) |
| The Sign Co. | https://www.thesignco.in | Full lifecycle service (design to installation), value engineering for budget clients, smart city as emerging market |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Product category grid is the primary navigation for print companies
- Portfolio/gallery is critical for signage companies (visual proof of work)
- Sample packs address the "can't touch" challenge of online print ordering
- Request for Quote (RFQ) forms instead of add-to-cart for custom/B2B products
- Manufacturing capabilities showcase builds confidence (CNC, offset, digital)
- Client logo bars are essential B2B trust signals
- Industry vertical targeting (retail, hospitality, corporate) helps B2B positioning
- Quality guarantee badges address print quality anxiety
- Personal point of contact differentiates from mass-market platforms
- Legacy/experience (25+ years, since 1967) is the primary trust signal

## Variant A — Print Shop / Digital Printing

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Shop name/logo, nav (Services, Products, Pricing, Portfolio, Contact), button ("Get a Quote").

2. **layout-row (Hero Split)**
   Left: heading ("Your Ideas, Perfectly Printed") + paragraph (fast turnaround, quality printing, competitive prices) + button ("Request a Quote"). Right: image (printed materials — brochures, banners, cards).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **columnsgrid (Services — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + caption. Business Cards, Brochures & Flyers, Banners & Signage, T-Shirt Printing, Stickers & Labels, Packaging, Large Format, Book Printing.

5. **tabs (Product Categories)**
   Tabs: Marketing Materials, Signage, Merchandise, Packaging, Stationery. Each: columnsgrid of products with image + heading + caption (quantity/price).

6. **layout-row (How It Works)**
   3-step: icon + heading + paragraph. "Upload Your Design (or use ours)" → "Review & Approve Proof" → "Print & Deliver".

7. **layout-row (Quick Pricing Guide)**
   accordion: Business Cards (100/250/500/1000 qty pricing), Flyers, Brochures, Banners — each with size options and price tiers.

8. **counter-up (Shop Stats)**
   "50,000+ Orders Completed", "Same-Day Available", "500+ Business Clients", "10+ Years".

9. **carousel (Portfolio)**
   Showcase of printed work: brochures, packaging, signage installations.

10. **layout-row (Design Services)**
   heading + paragraph (in-house design team, logo design, layout) + button ("Need a Design?").

11. **ticker (Trusted By)**
    Scrolling logos of regular business clients.

12. **form (Quote Request)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (product type), textbox (quantity), dropdown (size), textarea (specifications — paper, colors, finishing), button.

13. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Shop address, hours, phone, email, file upload instructions, social.

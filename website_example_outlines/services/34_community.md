# Community — Churches / Temples / Community Centers

> Focus: Connection & Welcome

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Elevation Church | elevationchurch.org | Welcoming hero video, sermon series, campus locations, online church |
| Life.Church | life.church | "Plan Your Visit" CTA, service times, groups/events, giving portal |
| Saddleback Church | saddleback.com | Campus selector, event calendar, sermon archive, connect groups |
| YMCA | ymca.org | Programs grid, location finder, membership tiers, impact stats |
| Jewish Community Center | jccsf.org | Events calendar, program categories, membership, donate button |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Warm, welcoming hero with "Plan Your Visit" or "Join Us" CTA
- Service/program times prominently displayed
- Events calendar or upcoming events section
- Community groups/ministries/programs grid
- Location and directions easily accessible
- Giving/donation integration

---

## Variant A — Church / Place of Worship

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Church name/logo, nav (About, Services, Ministries, Events, Give, Contact), button ("Plan Your Visit").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (congregation / worship / welcoming exterior), overlay: heading ("A Place to Belong") + paragraph (welcoming message — all are welcome, come as you are) + two buttons ("Plan Your Visit", "Watch Online").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **layout-row (Service Times)**
   heading ("Join Us This Week") + columnsgrid (3 cols): icon + heading + caption. Sunday Morning (9am & 11am), Wednesday Bible Study (7pm), Youth Group (Fridays 6pm).

5. **columnsgrid (Ministries — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph + button. Children's Ministry, Youth Group, Women's Fellowship, Men's Group, Music & Worship, Community Outreach.

6. **layout-row (Upcoming Events)**
   heading + columnsgrid (3 cols): image + heading + caption (date, time) + button ("Learn More"). Featured upcoming events.

7. **layout-row (Our Pastor)**
   Left: image (pastor / leader). Right: heading (name) + eyebrow (title) + paragraph (brief bio, vision for the community) + button ("Meet Our Team").

8. **counter-up (Community Impact)**
   "500+ Members", "12 Ministries", "20+ Community Events/Year", "15 Years Serving".

9. **carousel (Community Stories)**
   blockquote + caption (member name).

10. **layout-row (New Here?)**
    Left: heading ("What to Expect") + paragraph (casual atmosphere, children's programs, service length, parking info). Right: image (welcoming scene).

11. **form (Connect With Us)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (I'm interested in: First Visit, Volunteering, Small Groups, Prayer Request), textarea (message), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, service times, social links, giving link, denomination/affiliation.

## Variant B — Community Center / Recreation Hub

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Center name/logo, nav (Programs, Schedule, Membership, Events, Contact), button ("Become a Member").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: heading ("Your Community Hub for Fun, Fitness & Learning") + paragraph (programs for all ages — sports, arts, education, events) + two buttons ("View Programs", "Membership Info"). Right: image (active community center / people enjoying programs).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **tabs (Programs by Age)**
   Tabs: Kids (5-12), Teens, Adults, Seniors. Each: columnsgrid of program cards with icon + heading + caption (schedule) + button.

5. **columnsgrid (Facilities — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + paragraph. Gymnasium, Swimming Pool, Art Studio, Computer Lab, Meeting Rooms, Outdoor Fields.

6. **layout-row (Weekly Schedule)**
   heading + tabs or columnsgrid showing daily programming: class name + time + location + instructor.

7. **counter-up (Center Stats)**
   "5,000+ Members", "50+ Weekly Classes", "30 Years Serving the Community", "All Ages Welcome".

8. **layout-row (Membership Plans)**
   columnsgrid (3 cols): heading + paragraph + counter-up (price) + button. Individual, Family, Senior. Badge ("Best Value") on Family.

9. **carousel (Upcoming Events)**
   Slides: image + heading + caption (date, time) + button ("Register").

10. **carousel (Member Testimonials)**
    blockquote + caption.

11. **form (Contact / Register)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (interest: Membership, Program Registration, Room Rental, General Inquiry), textarea (message), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, hours, social links, city/county affiliation.

## Variant C — Volunteer Organization

**Page: Home (Single Page — Mission-Driven)**

1. **titlebar**
   Organization name/logo, nav (Our Mission, Programs, Volunteer, Events, Donate), button ("Volunteer Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (volunteers in action), overlay: heading ("Making a Difference, Together") + paragraph (mission statement — brief, inspiring) + two buttons ("Get Involved", "Donate").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **counter-up (Impact Numbers)**
   "10,000+ Volunteer Hours", "50+ Programs", "5,000 Lives Impacted", "200+ Active Volunteers".

5. **columnsgrid (Our Programs — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph + button. Food Bank, Tutoring & Mentoring, Senior Care, Habitat Builds, Environmental Cleanup, Holiday Drives.

6. **layout-row (How to Get Involved)**
   3-step: icon + heading + paragraph. "Sign Up Online" → "Attend Orientation" → "Make an Impact".

7. **carousel (Volunteer Stories)**
   image + blockquote + caption (volunteer name, program).

8. **layout-row (Upcoming Events)**
   heading + columnsgrid (3 cols): image + heading + caption (date) + button ("Sign Up").

9. **layout-row (Our Partners)**
   ticker of partner/sponsor logos.

10. **progress-bar (Fundraising Goals)**
    Current campaigns with progress bars: Food Drive (75%), Building Fund (40%), Scholarship Fund (90%).

11. **form (Sign Up to Volunteer)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (area of interest), dropdown (availability: Weekdays, Weekends, Evenings, Flexible), textarea (skills or experience), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, email, social links, 501(c)(3) registration number, donation link.

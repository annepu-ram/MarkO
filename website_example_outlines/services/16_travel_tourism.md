# Travel & Tourism — Tour Operators / Homestays

> Focus: Inspiration & Booking

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| SOTC | https://www.sotc.in | Destination carousel hero with pricing, theme-based tour categorization, 75 years trust, multi-service (tours + forex + visa) |
| Thomas Cook India | https://www.thomascook.in | Multi-service platform, search hero with tabs, "starting from" pricing on cards, forex as additional revenue |
| Hidden Treasures of India | https://htoindia.com | Inbound operator targeting international visitors, government credential badges, TripAdvisor integration, personal coordinator USP |
| Bout India | https://www.boutindia.com | Luxury positioning with premium imagery, "Travel Moods" categorization, detailed trip enquiry form with budget |
| Homestays of India | https://www.homestaysofindia.com | Region-based navigation, two-sided platform (guests + hosts), "supporting local livelihoods" social mission, host profiles |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Destination imagery hero is universal (full-width, high-quality photos)
- Package cards with pricing ("starting from") drive comparison and booking
- Theme/mood categorization helps diverse audiences find relevant tours
- Testimonials with trip photos are more persuasive than text-only
- Multi-service platforms (tours + forex + visa + insurance) maximize revenue
- Government credentials and TripAdvisor badges are essential trust signals
- Region/destination-first navigation over service-first
- Trip enquiry forms capture planning details (dates, interests, group size, budget)
- Seasonal offers create urgency

## Variant A — Tour Operator / Travel Agency

**Page 1: Home**

1. **titlebar**
   Agency name/logo, nav (Destinations, Packages, About, Testimonials, Contact), button ("Plan My Trip").

2. **carousel (Hero — Destination Showcase)**
   Full-width stunning destination images with overlay: heading (destination name) + caption (package highlight) + button ("Explore").

3. **counter-up (Travel Stats)**
   "10,000+ Happy Travelers", "100+ Destinations", "15+ Years", "4.8 Rating".

4. **columnsgrid (Popular Destinations — 4 columns)**
   Each: image + heading (destination) + caption (starting price, duration) + button ("View Package"). Goa, Kerala, Rajasthan, Kashmir, Ladakh, Andaman, Himachal, Northeast.

5. **tabs (Package Types)**
   Tabs: Honeymoon, Family, Adventure, Pilgrimage, Corporate, International. Each: 3-4 package cards with image + details + price + button.

6. **layout-row (Why Travel With Us)**
   columnsgrid (4 cols): icon + heading + caption. Customizable Itineraries, 24/7 Support, Best Price Guarantee, Trusted Hotel Partners, Visa Assistance, Travel Insurance.

7. **layout-row (Featured Package)**
   Large image + heading + paragraph (detailed itinerary preview) + counter-up (price) + countdown ("Limited Offer Ends:") + button ("Book Now").

8. **ticker (Hotel & Airline Partners)**
   Scrolling partner logos.

9. **carousel (Traveler Stories)**
   Travel photos + blockquote + rating + caption (traveler, destination).

10. **form (Trip Enquiry)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (destination/type), calendar (travel dates), textbox (number of travelers), dropdown (budget range), textarea (special requirements), button.

11. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Office address, phone, email, social links, IATA/tourism board affiliations.

**Page 2: Destination Detail**

1. **carousel** (destination gallery)
2. **tabs** (itinerary by day)
3. **accordion** (inclusions, exclusions, FAQs)
4. **form** (book this package)

## Variant B — Homestay / Farmstay / Villa Rental

**Page: Home (Single Page — Experiential)**

1. **titlebar**
   Property name, nav (Rooms, Experience, Gallery, Location, Book), button ("Check Availability").

2. **video-background (Hero)**
   Scenic property footage (sunset, nature, cozy interiors), overlay: heading ("Escape to Tranquility") + paragraph + button ("Book Your Stay").

3. **layout-row (The Experience)**
   Left: heading + paragraph (what makes this homestay unique — nature, local culture, home-cooked meals). Right: image (charming property exterior).

4. **tabs (Our Rooms)**
   Tab per room type: Deluxe, Cottage, Tree House, Family Suite. Each: carousel of room images + heading + paragraph (amenities, capacity) + counter-up (price/night) + button.

5. **columnsgrid (Activities & Experiences — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + caption. Nature Walks, Birdwatching, Bonfire, Local Cooking Class, Farm Tour, Cycling, Stargazing, River Rafting.

6. **layout-row (Local Cuisine)**
   image (home-cooked meal) + heading + paragraph (traditional recipes, fresh ingredients, special diets accommodated).

7. **carousel (Guest Gallery)**
   Guest photos and property beauty shots.

8. **counter-up (Guest Love)**
   "2,000+ Guests Hosted", "4.9 on TripAdvisor", "5+ Acres of Nature", "100% Organic Meals".

9. **carousel (Guest Reviews)**
   blockquote + rating + caption (guest, city).

10. **layout-row (Getting Here)**
    Image/map + heading + paragraph (directions from nearest city/airport, transport options).

11. **accordion (Stay Information)**
    Check-in/out times, meals included, cancellation policy, pet policy, what to pack.

12. **form (Book Your Stay)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), calendar (check-in), calendar (check-out), dropdown (room type), textbox (number of guests), textarea (special requests), button.

13. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, WhatsApp, email, booking platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com links), social.

7. **counter-up (Adventure Stats)**

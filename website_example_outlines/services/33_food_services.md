# Food Services — Catering / Food Trucks / Breweries

> Focus: Visual Appeal & Booking

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Proof Bread | proofbread.com | Full-screen food photography, minimal text, menu as scrolling list |
| Off the Grid | offthegrid.com | Event calendar, truck locator map, vendor profiles |
| Lagunitas | lagunitas.com | Brewery personality, taproom events, beer finder, bold typography |
| Bi-Rite Catering | biritesfcatering.com | Menu showcase, event type tabs, inquiry form |
| Kogi BBQ | kogibbq.com | Truck schedule, social integration, brand story |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Hero with mouth-watering food photography
- Menu or service packages displayed as cards or tabs
- Event/location schedules for mobile vendors
- Inquiry forms with event details (date, guest count, budget)
- Social media integration (Instagram feed)
- Taproom/tasting hours and event calendars

## Variant A — Catering Company

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Company name/logo, nav (Menus, Services, Gallery, Testimonials, Contact), button ("Request a Quote").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (beautifully plated spread), overlay: heading ("Exceptional Catering for Every Occasion") + paragraph (weddings, corporate, private events) + two buttons ("View Menus", "Get a Quote").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **counter-up (Track Record)**
   "2,000+ Events Catered", "15 Years Experience", "4.9 Rating", "Custom Menus".

5. **tabs (Our Menus)**
   Tabs: Wedding, Corporate, Social, Cocktail & Appetizers. Each: columnsgrid of dishes with images + captions + paragraph (menu highlights, pricing starting from).

6. **columnsgrid (Services — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph. Full-Service Catering, Drop-Off Catering, Staffing & Service, Bar & Beverage, Dessert & Cake, Event Planning Assistance.

7. **layout-row (How It Works)**
   4-step: icon + heading + caption. "Tell Us About Your Event" → "Menu Tasting" → "Event Day Setup" → "Seamless Service".

8. **carousel (Event Gallery)**
   Slides: event images with caption (event type, venue, guest count).

9. **carousel (Client Testimonials)**
   rating + blockquote + caption (client name, event type).

10. **ticker (Trusted By)**
    Scrolling logos: corporate clients, venues, wedding planners.

11. **form (Request a Quote)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (event type), calendar (event date), textbox (guest count), dropdown (budget range), textarea (menu preferences / dietary needs), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Phone, email, kitchen address, service area, social links (Instagram), health department license.

## Variant B — Food Truck / Mobile Kitchen

**Page: Home (Single Page — Fun & Energetic)**

1. **titlebar**
   Truck name/logo, nav (Menu, Schedule, Book Us, About, Contact), badge ("Order Ahead"), button ("Find Us Today").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: heading ("Street Food Done Right") + paragraph (gourmet tacos/burgers/BBQ — find us around the city) + two buttons ("Today's Location", "Book for Your Event"). Right: image (food truck in action / signature dish).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **columnsgrid (Our Menu — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + paragraph + caption (price). Signature items with descriptions.

5. **layout-row (Weekly Schedule)**
   heading + columnsgrid (5-7 cols): heading (day) + caption (location) + caption (hours). Monday through Sunday locations.

6. **counter-up (Fun Stats)**
   "50,000+ Meals Served", "150+ Events", "4.8 Stars", "3 Years on the Road".

7. **layout-row (Book Our Truck)**
   Left: heading ("Bring the Flavor to Your Event") + paragraph (weddings, corporate, festivals, private parties) + button ("Book Now"). Right: image (truck at an event).

8. **carousel (Customer Love)**
   rating + blockquote + caption.

9. **ticker (Events We've Done)**
   Scrolling: event names, festival logos, corporate logos.

10. **form (Book for Your Event)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), calendar (event date), textbox (guest count), dropdown (event type), textarea (details), button.

11. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Phone, email, social links (Instagram, TikTok), food truck permit info.

## Variant C — Craft Brewery / Winery

**Page: Home (Single Page — Brand & Experience)**

1. **titlebar**
   Brewery name/logo, nav (Beers, Taproom, Events, Our Story, Visit), badge ("Taproom Open"), button ("Plan Your Visit").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (taproom interior or pour shot), overlay: heading ("Craft Beer Brewed with Purpose") + paragraph (small-batch, locally sourced, taproom & events) + two buttons ("Our Beers", "Visit the Taproom").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **columnsgrid (Our Beers — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + paragraph + badge (style: IPA, Stout, Lager, etc.). Flagship and seasonal brews.

5. **layout-row (Our Story)**
   Left: image (founders / brewing process). Right: eyebrow ("Est. 2018") + heading ("From Garage to Taproom") + paragraph (origin story, brewing philosophy) + button ("Read More").

6. **tabs (Taproom & Events)**
   Tab 1 — Taproom: hours, location, food menu, live music schedule. Tab 2 — Private Events: party packages, buyouts, catering.

7. **counter-up (Brewery Stats)**
   "24 Beers on Tap", "5,000+ Barrels/Year", "200+ Taproom Events", "100% Independent".

8. **carousel (Taproom Gallery)**
   Slides: taproom photos, events, live music, food pairings.

9. **layout-row (Visit Us)**
   Left: heading + paragraph (address, hours, parking, dog-friendly). Right: image (taproom exterior or map placeholder).

10. **carousel (Reviews)**
    rating + blockquote + caption.

11. **form (Plan a Private Event)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), calendar (preferred date), dropdown (event type), textbox (guest count), textarea (details), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, taproom hours, phone, social links (Instagram, Untappd), age verification notice.

# Automotive — Garages / Car Wash / Detailing

> Focus: Convenience & Trust

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| The Detailing Mafia | https://www.thedetailingmafia.com | Dark premium aesthetic, brand partner logos (Labocosmetica, Nano Graphene), before/after gallery, franchise CTA, outlet locator |
| CarzSpa | https://www.carzspa.com | Proprietary product branding, franchise model, experience stats (since 2006), club/community engagement |
| Detailing Bull | https://detailingbull.com | Bike + car detailing, 23 locations as prominent stat, city-specific pages for local SEO |
| MyRaasta | https://myraasta.in | Vehicle selector (brand/model/fuel) as primary conversion, how-it-works steps, doorstep service, garage aggregator model |
| Detailing Dons | https://www.detailingdons.com | Pricing transparency with tiered packages, interactive before/after slider, franchise as growth model |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Dark premium aesthetic with high-contrast imagery is industry standard for detailing
- Before/after galleries are the primary trust and conversion tool
- Service-based pricing cards with tier comparison drive decisions
- Franchise model is extremely common for detailing chains
- Brand partner logos (coating brands) build technical credibility
- Vehicle selector/form is the primary conversion tool for repair platforms
- Counter stats (cars serviced, locations, years) are universal
- Multi-location outlet locator is essential for chains

## Variant A — Auto Repair Garage / Service Center

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Garage name/logo, nav (Services, Pricing, About, Reviews, Contact), badge ("Open 7 Days"), button ("Book Service").

2. **layout-row (Hero Split)**
   Left: heading ("Expert Car Care You Can Trust") + paragraph (all makes & models, certified mechanics) + two buttons ("Book a Service", "Call Now"). Right: image (mechanic working on car).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **counter-up (Trust Stats)**
   "15,000+ Vehicles Serviced", "20+ Years Experience", "4.8 Google Rating", "90-Day Warranty".

5. **columnsgrid (Services — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph + caption (starting price). General Service, Engine Repair, Brake & Suspension, AC Repair, Electrical, Transmission, Body Work, Tyre & Alignment.

6. **tabs (By Vehicle Type)**
   Tabs: Cars, SUVs, Commercial Vehicles, Vintage/Classic. Each: specific services and expertise.

7. **layout-row (Transparent Pricing)**
   heading + paragraph ("No hidden charges") + accordion: service packages with detailed pricing breakdown.

8. **layout-row (Our Workshop)**
   carousel of workshop images + heading + paragraph (equipment, lifts, diagnostic tools).

9. **layout-row (Certified & Trusted)**
   columnsgrid (4 cols): icon + caption. Certified Mechanics, Genuine Parts, Warranty on Repairs, Digital Job Cards.

10. **carousel (Customer Reviews)**
   rating + blockquote + caption (customer, car model).

11. **layout-row (Emergency Roadside)**
    heading + paragraph + button ("Call for Roadside Help") + badge ("24/7 Available").

12. **form (Book a Service)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (car make/model), textbox (registration number), dropdown (service type), calendar (preferred date), textarea (problem description), button.

13. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, hours, Google Maps, social links.

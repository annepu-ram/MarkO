# Beauty & Wellness — Salons / Spas / Parlours

> Focus: Repeat Bookings

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Lakme Salon | https://www.lakmesalon.in/ | Brand-first aesthetic, appointment booking as primary CTA, before/after transformations, store locator for franchise chain |
| Studieo7 Salon | https://studieo7.in/ | Technology differentiator (Metaverse), franchise promotion on homepage, Google reviews integration |
| Looks Salon | https://www.lookssalon.in/ | Chain model with 162+ branch count as proof, salon locator critical, franchise recruitment, celebrity clientele |
| Akasa Wellness | https://www.akasa.in/ | Luxury spa with earth tones, membership tiers, gift card promotion, treatment pricing transparency, Ayurveda integration |
| Sohum Spas | https://sohumspas.com/ | Location-centric with multiple cities, premium calm aesthetic, membership/loyalty programs |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Appointment booking CTA should be prominent and repeated throughout the page
- Before/after transformation galleries are the primary social proof for salons
- Serene/calming aesthetics for spas (earth tones, minimal) vs high-fashion for salons
- Store/salon locator is critical for chain businesses
- Franchise recruitment section is common for salon chains
- Membership plans and gift cards are key revenue drivers for spas
- Treatment menus with transparent pricing build trust
- Celebrity endorsements and Google reviews integration

## Variant A — Hair & Beauty Salon

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Salon name/logo, nav (Services, Pricing, Gallery, About, Book), button ("Book Appointment").

2. **layout-row (Hero Split)**
   Left: heading ("Where Beauty Meets Artistry") + paragraph (signature services, luxurious experience) + button ("Book Now"). Right: image (styled client / salon interior).

3. **ticker (Services Quick View)**
   Scrolling service badges: Haircuts, Coloring, Bridal Makeup, Facials, Manicure, Spa, Threading, Waxing.

4. **tabs (Services & Pricing)**
   Tabs: Hair, Skin, Nails, Bridal, Spa & Massage. Each tab: columnsgrid of services — heading (service name) + caption (duration) + counter-up (price).

5. **layout-row (Meet Our Stylists)**
   columnsgrid (3-4 cols): image + heading (name) + caption (specialization) + badge ("Lead Stylist").

6. **carousel (Transformations Gallery)**
   Before/after photos or styled look images.

7. **layout-row (Membership Plans)**
   3 cards: Silver / Gold / Platinum. Each: heading + paragraph (monthly services included) + counter-up (price/month) + badge ("Most Popular") + button.

8. **counter-up (Happy Clients)**
   "10,000+ Clients", "5 Star Rating", "8+ Expert Stylists", "7 Years of Excellence".

9. **carousel (Client Reviews)**
   rating + blockquote + caption.

10. **layout-row (Gift Cards CTA)**
    heading + paragraph ("The perfect gift") + image (gift card mockup) + button ("Buy Gift Card").

11. **form (Book Appointment)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), dropdown (service category), dropdown (stylist preference), calendar (date), dropdown (time slot), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, hours (Mon-Sun), phone, WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Maps link.

## Variant B — Day Spa / Wellness Center

**Page: Home (Single Page — Serene & Premium)**

1. **titlebar**
   Spa name, nav (Treatments, Packages, About, Wellness Blog, Book), button ("Reserve Your Escape").

2. **video-background (Hero)**
   Calming spa footage (candles, massage, water), overlay: heading ("Find Your Inner Peace") + paragraph + button ("Explore Treatments").

3. **br (wave divider)**

4. **columnsgrid (Signature Treatments — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + paragraph + caption (duration + price) + button. Swedish Massage, Aromatherapy, Hot Stone, Facial, Body Wrap, Reflexology.

5. **tabs (Spa Packages)**
   Tabs: Relaxation (1 hr), Rejuvenation (2 hrs), Ultimate Escape (Half Day), Couples Retreat. Each: heading + paragraph (treatments included) + counter-up (price) + button.

6. **layout-row (The Space)**
   carousel of spa interior images + heading + paragraph (ambiance, rooms, facilities).

7. **layout-row (Our Therapists)**
   columnsgrid: image + heading + caption (certification, specialty).

8. **counter-up (Wellness Numbers)**
   "5,000+ Spa Sessions", "100% Organic Products", "4.9 Rating", "Certified Therapists".

9. **carousel (Guest Testimonials)**
   blockquote + rating + caption.

10. **accordion (Spa Etiquette & FAQs)**
    Arrival time, what to wear, cancellation policy, health conditions, gift certificates.

11. **form (Book a Treatment)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), dropdown (treatment/package), dropdown (therapist preference), calendar (date), dropdown (time), checkbox (couples session), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, hours, phone, email, social links, products we use (brand logos).

# Healthcare — Small Clinics / Specialists

> Focus: Trust & Patient Care

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Apollo 24\|7 | apollo247.com | Search-first hero, doctor profiles with ratings |
| Apollo Clinic | apolloclinic.com | Clinic locator, health check packages with pricing |
| Clove Dental | clovedental.in | Inline appointment form in hero, before/after gallery, transparent pricing |
| Medanta | medanta.org | Emergency number in top bar, video testimonials, awards logo bar |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Inline appointment form in hero section reduces friction
- Before/After gallery uniquely powerful for dental/cosmetic
- FAQ accordion addresses patient objections on homepage
- Doctor credentials + ratings build credibility
- Clinic locator critical for multi-location businesses
- Health check packages with transparent pricing

## Variant A — Single-Page Clinic (Doctor's Practice)

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Clinic name/logo, nav links (Services, About, Book, Contact), phone number CTA button.

2. **layout-row (Hero Split)**
   Left column: heading + paragraph (clinic tagline, "Compassionate care for your family"), button ("Book Appointment"). Right column: image of doctor/clinic.

3. **br (divider)**
   Subtle line separating hero from services.

4. **columnsgrid (Services Grid — 3 columns)**
   Each column: icon + heading + paragraph. Services like General Checkup, Pediatrics, Dermatology, Women's Health, Lab Tests, Vaccinations.

5. **layout-row (About the Doctor)**
   Left: image (doctor portrait). Right: heading + paragraph (credentials, experience, philosophy), badge ("15+ Years Experience").

6. **accordion (FAQs)**
   Common patient questions: insurance accepted, walk-in vs appointment, clinic hours, what to bring.

7. **counter-up (Trust Stats Row)**
   layout-row with 3-4 counter-ups: "10,000+ Patients Treated", "15+ Years Experience", "4.8 Star Rating", "98% Satisfaction".

8. **carousel (Patient Testimonials)**
   Slides with blockquote + paragraph (patient reviews), rating stars.

9. **columnsgrid (Insurance Partners)**
   Logo images of accepted insurance providers in a 4-6 column grid.

10. **layout-row (Book Appointment CTA)**
    heading + paragraph + button ("Schedule Your Visit Today").

11. **layout-row (Footer)**
    3 columns: clinic address + map link, operating hours, phone/email/social links.

## Variant B — Multi-Page Specialist Clinic (Dental / Eye / Ortho)

**Page 1: Home**

1. **titlebar**
   Clinic logo, nav (Home, Specializations, Doctors, Testimonials, Contact), "Book Now" button.

2. **video-background (Hero)**
   Clinic interior video, overlay with heading ("Advanced Eye Care You Can Trust"), paragraph, button ("Explore Our Services").

3. **ticker (Certifications/Awards)**
   Scrolling badges/icons of accreditations, awards, affiliations.

4. **tabs (Specializations)**
   Tab per specialty: Cataract Surgery, LASIK, Glaucoma Treatment, Retina Care. Each tab: image + heading + paragraph + button.

5. **columnsgrid (Why Choose Us — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + caption. Differentiators: Latest Equipment, Experienced Surgeons, Affordable Plans, Post-Op Care.

6. **layout-row (Lead Doctor Profile)**
   image + heading + paragraph (chief doctor bio, qualifications), badge ("Chief Surgeon").

7. **rating (Google Rating Display)**
   Star rating with review count.

8. **carousel (Before/After or Testimonials)**
   Patient success stories or visual results.

9. **countdown (Limited Offer)**
   "Free Eye Checkup Camp — Ends In:" with countdown timer + button to register.

10. **form (Quick Appointment)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), dropdown (service), calendar (preferred date), button (submit).

11. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, map embed, emergency number, social links.

**Page 2: Doctors**

1. **columnsgrid (Doctor Profiles — 3 columns)**
   Each: image, heading (name), caption (specialty), paragraph (brief bio), button ("View Profile").

**Page 3: Contact**

1. **layout-row**
   Left: form (name, email, phone, textarea for message). Right: image of clinic exterior, address, map link.

## Variant C — Multi-Doctor Polyclinic / Health Center

**Page: Home (Single Page, Content-Heavy)**

1. **titlebar**
   Health center logo, hamburger menu for mobile, nav links, emergency phone badge.

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image background, heading ("Your Family's Health, Our Priority"), paragraph, two buttons ("Book Appointment", "Emergency: Call Now").

3. **br (wave divider)**

4. **columnsgrid (Departments — 3x2 grid)**
   Each: icon + heading + caption. Departments: General Medicine, Pediatrics, Gynecology, Orthopedics, ENT, Dermatology.

5. **tabs (Our Doctors by Department)**
   Tab per department, each showing a columnsgrid of doctor cards (image, name, credentials, availability).

6. **layout-row (Health Packages)**
   Cards in a row: badge ("Popular") + heading + paragraph (package details) + counter-up (price) + button ("Book Package").

7. **accordion (Patient Information)**
   Items: How to book, Insurance & billing, Visiting hours, Patient rights, Pre-visit checklist.

8. **ticker (Patient Reviews)**
   Scrolling testimonial cards with rating + blockquote.

9. **progress-bar (Patient Satisfaction Metrics)**
   Multiple bars: "Overall Satisfaction 96%", "Wait Time <15 min 88%", "Would Recommend 94%".

10. **layout-row (Newsletter + Contact)**
    Left: heading + paragraph + form (email + button for health tips newsletter). Right: address, phone, hours, map link.

11. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Multi-column: departments list, quick links, contact info, social media icons.

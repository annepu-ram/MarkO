## Variant A — HVAC / AC Service & Repair

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Company name/logo, nav (Services, Areas, Pricing, Reviews, Contact), badge ("24/7 Emergency"), button ("Call Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: heading ("Fast, Reliable AC Repair & Service") + paragraph (same-day service, certified technicians) + two buttons ("Book Service", "Emergency: Call Now"). Right: image (technician at work).

3. **counter-up (Trust Stats)**
   "10,000+ Services Completed", "1-Hour Response Time", "4.8 Star Rating", "5+ Years Warranty".

4. **columnsgrid (Services — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph + button. AC Repair, AC Installation, Annual Maintenance, Duct Cleaning, Refrigeration, Heating Systems.

5. **layout-row (Why Choose Us)**
   columnsgrid (4 cols): icon + heading + caption. Certified Technicians, Same-Day Service, Transparent Pricing, Genuine Parts.

6. **tabs (Service Plans)**
   Tabs: One-Time Service, Annual AMC Basic, Annual AMC Premium. Each: heading + paragraph (inclusions) + counter-up (price) + button.

7. **layout-row (How It Works)**
   3-step: icon + heading + paragraph. "Book Online/Call" → "Technician Arrives" → "Problem Solved".

8. **carousel (Customer Reviews)**
   Slides: rating + blockquote + caption (customer, location).

9. **layout-row (Areas We Serve)**
   heading + columnsgrid of area badges/names + paragraph ("Serving all of [City] and surrounding areas").

10. **form (Book a Service)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (address), dropdown (service type), dropdown (AC brand), calendar (preferred date), dropdown (time slot), textarea (problem description), button.

11. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Phone (prominent), WhatsApp, email, service areas, working hours, license numbers.

## Variant B — Pest Control Service

**Page: Home (Single Page — Urgency-Focused)**

1. **titlebar**
   Company logo, nav (Services, Residential, Commercial, Pricing, Contact), badge ("Same Day Service"), button ("Get Free Inspection").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (clean home), overlay: heading ("Pest-Free Living, Guaranteed") + paragraph + two buttons ("Book Free Inspection", "Call: [Number]").

3. **ticker (Pests We Handle)**
   Scrolling: icon + caption for each pest type — Cockroaches, Termites, Bed Bugs, Mosquitoes, Rats, Ants, Spiders.

4. **columnsgrid (Services — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph + button. General Pest Control, Termite Treatment, Bed Bug Treatment, Mosquito Fogging, Rodent Control, Commercial Fumigation.

5. **tabs (Residential vs Commercial)**
   Tab 1 — Residential: services, pricing tiers, warranty info. Tab 2 — Commercial: industries served (restaurants, warehouses, offices), compliance.

6. **layout-row (Our Process)**
   4-step: icon + heading + caption. "Free Inspection" → "Custom Treatment Plan" → "Safe Application" → "Follow-Up & Warranty".

7. **counter-up (Results)**
   "20,000+ Homes Treated", "100% Safe Chemicals", "6-Month Warranty", "4.9 Rating".

8. **layout-row (Pricing Plans)**
   3 cards: One-Time / Quarterly / Annual. Each: heading + paragraph + counter-up (price) + badge ("Best Value" on Annual) + button.

9. **carousel (Before/After + Reviews)**
   Testimonials with before/after context.

10. **accordion (FAQs)**
    Safety of chemicals, pet-friendly options, how long treatment takes, warranty terms, preparation before treatment.

11. **form (Get Free Inspection)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (address/area), dropdown (pest type), dropdown (property type), checkbox (urgent/same-day), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Phone, WhatsApp, areas served, license number, safety certifications.

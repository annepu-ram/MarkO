# Exhibition / Trade Expo / Art Show

> Focus: Visual discovery, exhibitor showcasing, and visitor registration

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Art Basel | artbasel.com | Full-bleed gallery imagery, artist/gallery directory, schedule tabs |
| CES | ces.tech | Exhibitor search, keynote speaker cards, badge registration flow |
| Canton Fair | cantonfair.org.cn | Exhibitor categories, floor plan imagery, buyer registration |
| Frieze | frieze.com | Editorial-meets-gallery layout, artist spotlights, ticket tiers |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Exhibitor/artist directory with filterable categories
- Floor plan or venue map imagery for wayfinding
- Schedule organized by day/track using tabs
- Countdown to opening day builds anticipation
- Sponsor/partner ticker for credibility
- Past event gallery proves scale and quality

## Variant A — Art Exhibition / Gallery Show

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Gallery/exhibition name, nav (Exhibition, Artists, Schedule, Tickets, Visit, Contact), button ("Get Tickets").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width background image (gallery interior with visitors viewing large artworks), overlay: eyebrow ("March 15 - April 30, 2026") + heading ("Visions of Tomorrow: Contemporary Art Exhibition") + paragraph ("Featuring 40+ artists from 12 countries") + button ("Buy Tickets") + button ("Meet the Artists").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Opening Night Countdown)**
   heading ("Opening Night In...") + countdown to exhibition opening date.

5. **layout-row (About the Exhibition)**
   Left: image (featured artwork or installation preview). Right: heading ("Curatorial Statement") + paragraph (exhibition theme, artistic vision, what visitors will experience) + caption ("Curated by [Name], [Institution]").

6. **tabs (Featured Artists)**
   Tabs: Painting, Sculpture, Digital Art, Photography, Mixed Media. Each tab: columnsgrid (3 columns) with image (artwork sample) + heading (artist name) + caption (country, medium) + button ("View Portfolio").

7. **carousel (Artwork Preview)**
   Large-format images of selected works with caption (title, artist, medium, year).

8. **layout-row (Exhibition Schedule)**
   heading ("Events & Programming") + columnsgrid (3 columns): icon + heading + caption + paragraph. "Opening Night Reception", "Artist Talk Series", "Guided Tours", "Workshops", "Closing Ceremony", "Family Day".

9. **columnsgrid (Ticket Options — 3 columns)**
   Each: heading ("General Admission", "VIP Preview", "Season Pass") + paragraph (access details) + counter-up (price) + button ("Purchase"). VIP gets badge ("Includes Opening Night").

10. **layout-row (Venue & Visit Info)**
   Left: image (venue exterior or map). Right: heading ("Plan Your Visit") + paragraph (address, hours, accessibility info) + icon + caption ("Free parking available") + button ("Get Directions").

11. **counter-up (Exhibition Stats)**
    "45 Artists", "120+ Artworks", "6 Weeks", "3 Gallery Halls".

12. **ticker (Partner & Sponsor Logos)**
    Scrolling logos of galleries, cultural institutions, and sponsors.

13. **form (Newsletter & Updates)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), checkbox (receive exhibition updates), checkbox (interested in artist talks), button ("Subscribe").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Gallery address, opening hours, phone, email, social media (Instagram prominent), accessibility info, press inquiries contact.

## Variant B — Trade Fair / Industry Expo

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Expo name/logo, nav (Exhibitors, Schedule, Speakers, Floor Plan, Register, Contact), button ("Register Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Background image (busy expo hall with booths and attendees), overlay: eyebrow ("Asia's Largest Manufacturing Expo") + heading ("TechIndustry Expo 2026") + paragraph ("500+ exhibitors, 50+ keynotes, 3 days of innovation") + button ("Register Free") + button ("Exhibitor Enquiry").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Expo Countdown)**
   heading ("Expo Opens In...") + countdown timer.

5. **counter-up (Expo Scale)**
   "500+ Exhibitors", "25,000 Visitors", "50+ Keynotes", "12 Industry Sectors".

6. **tabs (Exhibitor Categories)**
   Tabs by sector: Manufacturing, Automation, Materials, Software, Logistics. Each tab: columnsgrid (4 columns) with image (company logo or product) + heading (company name) + caption (booth number) + button ("Visit Booth").

7. **layout-row (Keynote Speakers)**
   heading ("Featured Speakers") + columnsgrid (4 columns): image (headshot) + heading (name) + caption (title, company) + paragraph (topic preview).

8. **columnsgrid (Conference Tracks — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading (track name: "Digital Transformation", "Sustainable Manufacturing", "Supply Chain Innovation") + paragraph (session count, key topics) + button ("View Sessions").

9. **layout-row (Floor Plan)**
   heading ("Exhibition Floor Plan") + image (venue floor map with hall labels) + paragraph (hall descriptions) + button ("Download Full Map").

10. **accordion (Visitor FAQ)**
   Sections: "How do I register?", "Is parking available?", "What are the opening hours?", "Can I attend specific days only?", "Is there a mobile app?".

11. **columnsgrid (Registration Tiers — 3 columns)**
    Each: heading ("Visitor Pass", "Conference Pass", "All-Access VIP") + paragraph (inclusions) + counter-up (price, visitor pass shows "$0 Free") + button ("Register"). Conference pass gets badge ("Best Value").

12. **ticker (Exhibitor Logos)**
    Scrolling logos of major exhibiting companies.

13. **form (Visitor Registration)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), textbox (company), textbox (job title), dropdown (industry sector), dropdown (pass type), checkbox (receive exhibitor updates), button ("Complete Registration").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Venue address, dates, organizer contact, exhibitor sales email, press/media inquiries, social media links, past expo archives.

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Exhibition name, nav (Exhibits, Schedule, Speakers, Workshops, Tickets, Visit), button ("Book Tickets").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**

Innovate.") + heading ("The National Science & Innovation Exhibition 2026") + paragraph ("Hands-on exhibits, live demonstrations, and the future of technology") + button ("Get Tickets") + button ("School Group Booking").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Exhibition Opens)**
   heading ("Doors Open In...") + countdown timer.

5. **layout-row (About the Exhibition)**
   Left: heading ("What to Expect") + paragraph (exhibition scope: robotics, space, biotech, renewable energy, AI) + counter-up ("200+ Interactive Exhibits"). Right: image (visitors interacting with a large-scale exhibit).

6. **columnsgrid (Exhibition Zones — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading (zone name: "Space & Astronomy", "Robotics Lab", "Green Energy Pavilion", "AI & Future Tech") + caption (number of exhibits) + paragraph (highlights) + button ("Explore Zone").

7. **tabs (Daily Schedule)**
   Tabs by day: Day 1, Day 2, Day 3, Day 4. Each tab: columnsgrid with icon + heading (session name) + caption (time, location) + paragraph (speaker or activity description).

8. **layout-row (Featured Speaker)**
   Left: image (scientist or innovator). Right: eyebrow ("Keynote Presentation") + heading (speaker name) + paragraph (credentials, topic) + caption ("Day 2, Main Auditorium, 2:00 PM") + button ("Add to Schedule").

9. **carousel (Exhibit Previews)**
   Photos and renders of key exhibits: robotic arm demos, VR experiences, renewable energy models, DNA sequencing displays.

10. **columnsgrid (Workshop Highlights — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading (workshop name: "Build Your Own Robot", "Code an AI", "3D Print Your Design") + caption (age group, duration) + paragraph (description) + badge ("Hands-On") + button ("Register").

11. **columnsgrid (Ticket Options — 3 columns)**
    Each: heading ("Adult", "Child (5-15)", "Family Pack (2+2)") + paragraph (access included) + counter-up (price) + button ("Buy Tickets"). Family pack gets badge ("Best Value").

12. **layout-row (Venue & Practical Info)**
    Left: image (venue exterior). Right: heading ("Getting Here") + paragraph (address, transport options, parking) + icon + caption ("Fully accessible") + button ("View Map").

13. **ticker (Knowledge Partners & Sponsors)**
    Scrolling logos of universities, research institutions, tech companies, and government bodies.

14. **form (School & Group Booking)**
    textbox (school/organization name), textbox (contact name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (group size range), calendar (preferred date), textarea (special requirements), button ("Submit Booking Request").

15. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Venue address, exhibition dates, opening hours, ticket office phone, email, social media, accessibility statement, press kit download link.

# Book Reading / Literary Festival / Book Launch

> Focus: Community-driven cultural events celebrating literature, authors, and the joy of reading

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Jaipur Literature Festival | jaipurliteraturefestival.org | Multi-day schedule tabs, speaker cards with bios, countdown to event |
| Hay Festival | hayfestival.com | Author lineup grid, event filtering by genre, ticket bundles |
| Brooklyn Book Festival | brooklynbookfestival.org | Clean schedule layout, featured author spotlights, volunteer signup |
| Penguin Random House Events | penguinrandomhouse.com/events | Author tour dates, book cover imagery, RSVP forms |
| LitHub | lithub.com | Editorial storytelling, author interviews, event listings with rich imagery |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Author headshots and book cover imagery are central visual elements
- Schedule/lineup is the most-visited section — make it prominent with tabs or accordion
- Countdown to event date builds urgency for ticket sales
- Blockquotes from authors or past attendees add literary credibility
- Genre-based filtering helps visitors find sessions they care about
- Simple RSVP or ticket form — literary audiences prefer straightforward flows

## Variant A — Book Launch Event (Single Book)

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Publisher or imprint logo, nav (About the Book, Author, Events, Press, RSVP), button ("Pre-Order Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: large image (book cover, beautifully styled on a table or held by the author). Right: eyebrow ("Coming March 15, 2026") + heading (book title) + paragraph (one-line hook from the jacket copy) + button ("Reserve Your Seat") + button ("Pre-Order").

3. **br (wave section breaker, color matching next section background)**
   Decorative wave divider transitioning from hero to content section.

4. **countdown (Launch Day)**
   Countdown timer to the launch event date: days, hours, minutes, seconds. Heading: "Until the Grand Launch".

5. **layout-row (About the Book)**
   Left: heading ("About the Book") + paragraph (2-3 sentence synopsis without spoilers) + blockquote (a powerful line from the book). Right: image (interior page spread or artistic illustration from the book).

6. **layout-row (Meet the Author)**
   Left: image (author portrait, warm and approachable). Right: heading (author name) + paragraph (bio — previous works, awards, background) + badge ("Bestselling Author") + link ("Visit Author Website").

7. **columnsgrid (Praise & Reviews — 3 columns)**
   Each: rating (4-5 stars) + blockquote (review excerpt) + caption (reviewer name, publication — e.g., "The New York Times", "Kirkus Reviews", "Goodreads Early Reader").

8. **layout-row (Launch Event Details)**
   Left: heading ("Join Us for the Launch") + paragraph (date, time, venue address, what to expect — reading, Q&A, signing) + icon (calendar icon) + caption (date) + icon (map pin) + caption (venue). Right: image (venue photo or past book launch event).

9. **accordion (Event Schedule)**
   Panels: "6:00 PM — Doors Open & Welcome Drinks", "6:30 PM — Author Reading (Chapters 1-3)", "7:00 PM — In Conversation With [Moderator Name]", "7:30 PM — Audience Q&A", "8:00 PM — Book Signing & Networking".

10. **carousel (Sneak Peek — Excerpts & Imagery)**
   Slides of styled book photography, quote cards from the book, behind-the-scenes author writing photos.

11. **ticker (Early Reader Reactions)**
    Scrolling cards: blockquote (short reaction) + caption (reader name, city).

12. **form (RSVP for the Launch)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), dropdown (number of guests: 1, 2, 3), checkbox ("I'd like a signed copy reserved"), textarea (any questions for the author), button ("Reserve My Spot").

13. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Publisher name and logo, contact email, social media links (Instagram, Twitter/X, Goodreads), link ("Media & Press Kit"), copyright notice.

## Variant B — Literary Festival / Book Fair

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Festival logo, nav (Lineup, Schedule, Venues, Tickets, Sponsors, Contact), button ("Buy Passes").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (festival grounds with tents, crowds, and book stalls) + overlay: eyebrow ("3rd Annual") + heading ("Riverside Literary Festival 2026") + paragraph ("Three days of readings, panels, and conversations with 50+ authors") + button ("View Full Schedule") + button ("Get Tickets").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **counter-up (Festival Stats)**
   "50+ Authors", "30 Sessions", "3 Days", "5,000 Attendees Last Year".

5. **countdown (Festival Begins)**
   Countdown to opening day with heading: "The Festival Begins In".

6. **tabs (Featured Authors by Genre)**
   Tabs: Fiction, Non-Fiction, Poetry, Children's. Each tab: columnsgrid (3 columns) with image (author headshot) + heading (author name) + caption (latest book title) + badge ("Keynote Speaker" on select authors).

7. **accordion (Daily Schedule)**
   Panels per day: "Day 1 — Friday, April 10" with paragraph listing time slots, session titles, author names, and venue/stage. "Day 2 — Saturday, April 11" and "Day 3 — Sunday, April 12" similarly detailed.

8. **columnsgrid (Venues & Stages — 3 columns)**
   Each: image (venue/stage photo) + heading (stage name — "Main Stage", "Poetry Tent", "Children's Corner") + caption (capacity, location description).

9. **layout-row (Ticket Packages)**
   columnsgrid (3 columns): Each: heading (pass type — "Day Pass", "Weekend Pass", "VIP All-Access") + paragraph (inclusions) + counter-up (price) + badge ("Best Value" on Weekend Pass) + button ("Buy Now").

10. **carousel (Past Festival Highlights)**
   Photos from previous years: packed audiences, author signings, panel discussions, outdoor reading sessions.

11. **ticker (Sponsor Logos)**
    Scrolling logos: major publishers, bookstores, media partners, cultural foundations.

12. **layout-row (Volunteer & Participate)**
    Left: heading ("Be Part of the Festival") + paragraph (volunteer roles, open mic opportunities, book swap info) + button ("Sign Up to Volunteer"). Right: image (volunteers at past festival).

13. **form (Newsletter & Updates)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), dropdown (interest: "Author Lineup Updates", "Ticket Alerts", "Volunteer Opportunities"), checkbox ("I agree to receive festival emails"), button ("Subscribe").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Festival dates and venue address, organizer contact, social media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X), link ("Press & Media"), link ("Code of Conduct"), sponsor acknowledgment row.

## Variant C — Author Reading Series / Book Club Event

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Book club or series name/logo, nav (Upcoming Readings, Past Events, Authors, Join Us, Contact), button ("Become a Member").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: heading ("Stories Told Live") + paragraph ("A monthly reading series bringing acclaimed authors to intimate venues across the city") + button ("Next Event") + button ("View All Readings"). Right: image (cozy bookshop or library setting with audience listening to an author).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **layout-row (Next Reading — Featured)**
   Left: image (upcoming author headshot). Right: eyebrow ("Next Reading — March 22, 2026") + heading (author name) + paragraph (book title, brief description of the reading) + caption (venue name, time) + badge ("Members Get Priority Seating") + button ("RSVP Now").

5. **columnsgrid (Upcoming Readings — 3 columns)**
   Each: image (author photo) + heading (author name) + caption (book title) + paragraph (date, venue) + button ("RSVP").

6. **layout-row (How It Works)**
   columnsgrid (4 columns): Each: icon + heading + caption. "Browse Upcoming Readings" + "RSVP Online" + "Attend & Enjoy" + "Discuss & Connect".

7. **tabs (Past Readings Archive)**
   Tabs by year or season: "Spring 2026", "Winter 2025", "Fall 2025". Each: columnsgrid with image (event photo) + heading (author name) + caption (book title, date) + link ("Watch Recording").

8. **carousel (Event Photos)**
   Intimate photos from past readings: authors at the podium, audience engagement, book signing tables, post-event conversations.

9. **layout-row (Membership Benefits)**
   Left: heading ("Join the Reading Circle") + paragraph (benefits — priority seating, signed copies, members-only Q&A sessions, annual reading list) + counter-up (membership price per year). Right: columnsgrid (2 columns) with icon + caption for each benefit.

10. **ticker (Reader Reviews)**
   Scrolling cards: blockquote (attendee feedback) + rating + caption (member name).

11. **blockquote (Featured Author Quote)**
    A standout quote from a past visiting author about the reading series experience, with caption (author name, book title).

12. **form (RSVP / Join Mailing List)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), dropdown (event: list of upcoming readings), radio (attendance type: "In-Person", "Virtual Livestream"), checkbox ("Add me to the monthly reading list newsletter"), button ("RSVP").

13. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Series organizer name, venue partner addresses, contact email, social media links (Instagram, Goodreads, YouTube for recorded readings), link ("Suggest an Author"), copyright.

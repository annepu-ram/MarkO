# Wedding Expo / Bridal Show / Wedding Fair

> Focus: Vendor discovery, inspiration browsing, and ticket-driven attendance for couples planning weddings

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| The Wedding Expo | theweddingexpo.co.za | Exhibitor directory, ticket CTA, countdown to event, floor plan |
| National Bridal Show | nationalbridalshow.com | Vendor categories, fashion show schedule, early bird pricing |
| Bride World Expo | brideworldexpo.com | City-based events, vendor spotlights, attendee registration form |
| The Bridal Fair | thebridalfair.com.au | Inspiration galleries, runway schedule, VIP experience upgrade |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Countdown timer to next event date drives urgency
- Vendor/exhibitor directory organized by category (florists, photographers, caterers, venues)
- Ticket tiers (General Admission vs VIP) with clear pricing
- Fashion show / runway schedule is a major draw
- Inspiration gallery showcasing past expos builds excitement
- Floor plan or exhibitor map helps attendees plan their visit
- Testimonials from past brides who found their vendors at the expo

# Live Entertainment / Comedy Show / Stand-Up

> Focus: Performer-driven, ticket urgency, and entertainment atmosphere

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Comedy Cellar | comedycellar.com | Lineup-first layout, show times grid, minimal friction ticket purchase |
| Second City | secondcity.com | Show carousel, performer bios, venue info, group booking |
| Broadway.com | broadway.com | Hero imagery, show schedule tabs, review quotes, ticket CTA everywhere |
| Eventbrite Comedy | eventbrite.com | Countdown urgency, social proof (X tickets left), lineup cards |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Performer/artist lineup is the main draw — headshots and credits prominent
- Show schedule with dates, times, and ticket availability
- Urgency messaging (limited seats, selling fast badges)
- Venue info with seating chart or capacity
- Video clips or past performance highlights build excitement
- Group booking and private event options for upsell

## Variant A — Stand-Up Comedy Show / Comedy Festival

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Show/festival name, nav (Lineup, Shows, Tickets, Venue, About, Contact), button ("Buy Tickets").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Background image (comedian on stage under spotlight with laughing audience), overlay: eyebrow ("Live Stand-Up Comedy") + heading ("Laugh Out Loud Comedy Festival 2026") + paragraph ("Three nights of the funniest comedians from across the country") + button ("Get Tickets") + button ("See the Lineup").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Show Countdown)**
   heading ("The Laughs Begin In...") + countdown timer to opening night.

5. **columnsgrid (Headliner Lineup — 3 columns)**
   Each: image (comedian headshot) + heading (name) + caption (credits: "As Seen on Netflix, Comedy Central") + paragraph (comedy style and notable bits) + badge ("Headliner") + button ("Watch Clip").

6. **tabs (Show Schedule)**
   Tabs: Friday Night, Saturday Night, Sunday Matinee. Each tab: columnsgrid with image (performer) + heading (name) + caption (set time) + badge ("Selling Fast" where applicable) + button ("Get Tickets").

7. **carousel (Performance Clips)**
   Video thumbnails or gif clips from past performances with caption (comedian name, show title).

8. **layout-row (The Festival Experience)**
   Left: heading ("More Than Just Comedy") + paragraph (food trucks, bar, meet-and-greet, merch booth) + icon + caption ("Full bar & food available"). Right: image (festival atmosphere, audience enjoying themselves).

9. **columnsgrid (Ticket Options — 3 columns)**
   Each: heading ("Single Show", "Weekend Pass", "VIP All-Access") + paragraph (inclusions: shows, seating priority, meet-and-greet, drink vouchers) + counter-up (price) + button ("Purchase"). Weekend pass gets badge ("Best Value").

10. **counter-up (Festival Stats)**
   "15 Comedians", "6 Shows", "3 Nights", "2,000+ Laughs Guaranteed".

11. **layout-row (Venue Info)**
    Left: image (venue interior showing stage and seating). Right: heading ("The Venue") + paragraph (name, address, capacity, seating style) + caption ("Intimate 300-seat theatre") + button ("View Seating Chart") + button ("Get Directions").

12. **ticker (As Featured In)**
    Scrolling media logos and review quotes: "Hilarious! - TimeOut", "Must-See - Comedy Weekly".

13. **accordion (FAQ)**
    Sections: "What is the age restriction?", "Is there a drink minimum?", "Can I get a refund?", "Are cameras allowed?", "Is the venue accessible?", "Group discounts?".

14. **form (Group Booking / Private Events)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (event type: birthday, corporate, bachelor/bachelorette), textbox (group size), calendar (preferred date), textarea (special requests), button ("Submit Enquiry").

15. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Venue address, box office phone, email, social media (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube), parking info, age policy.

## Variant B — Theatre / Drama Production

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Theatre company name, nav (The Show, Cast, Schedule, Tickets, Venue, Contact), button ("Book Seats").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width background image (dramatic stage scene with actors in costume), overlay: eyebrow ("A Stirring New Production") + heading ("The Glass Menagerie") + paragraph ("Tennessee Williams' masterpiece, reimagined for a new generation") + caption ("Directed by [Name]") + button ("Book Now") + button ("Watch Trailer").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **layout-row (About the Production)**
   Left: heading ("The Story") + paragraph (plot synopsis without spoilers, themes, why this production matters) + blockquote ("A hauntingly beautiful revival" - Theatre Review). Right: image (production still showing key dramatic moment).

5. **columnsgrid (Cast & Crew — 4 columns)**
   Each: image (headshot in costume) + heading (character name) + caption (actor name) + paragraph (actor credits and background).

6. **layout-row (Director's Vision)**
   Left: image (director portrait). Right: eyebrow ("Director's Note") + heading (director name) + blockquote (statement about creative vision and interpretation) + caption (directing credits).

7. **tabs (Show Dates)**
   Tabs by week or month. Each tab: columnsgrid with heading (date) + caption (time: "7:30 PM" or "2:00 PM Matinee") + badge ("Few Seats Left" or "Added Show") + button ("Book").

8. **carousel (Production Gallery)**
   High-quality rehearsal and production photos capturing key scenes and moments.

9. **columnsgrid (Seating & Pricing — 3 columns)**
   Each: heading ("Stalls", "Dress Circle", "Upper Circle") + paragraph (view description, legroom notes) + counter-up (price from) + button ("Select Seats").

10. **layout-row (Reviews & Press)**
   heading ("What Critics Are Saying") + columnsgrid (3 columns): rating + blockquote (review excerpt) + caption (publication name).

11. **counter-up (Production Numbers)**
    "24 Performances", "4 Weeks Only", "850-Seat Theatre", "5-Star Reviews".

12. **accordion (Visitor Information)**
    Sections: "Running Time & Interval", "Age Suitability", "Accessibility & Assisted Performances", "Latecomers Policy", "Dining & Pre-Show Drinks".

13. **form (Group Booking)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), textbox (group size), dropdown (preferred performance), checkbox (require accessible seating), checkbox (interested in pre-show talk), textarea (additional requirements), button ("Request Group Booking").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Theatre address, box office hours and phone, email, social media, season brochure link, education program info.

## Variant C — Variety Show / Talent Competition

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Show name, nav (Performers, Schedule, Judges, Tickets, Auditions, Contact), button ("Get Tickets").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Background image (colorful stage with multiple performers — singer, dancer, magician), overlay: eyebrow ("The Ultimate Talent Showcase") + heading ("StarStage Variety Show 2026") + paragraph ("Singers, dancers, comedians, magicians, and extraordinary acts compete for the grand prize") + button ("Buy Tickets") + button ("Apply to Perform").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Show Night Countdown)**
   heading ("The Show Starts In...") + countdown timer.

5. **layout-row (About the Show)**
   Left: heading ("What Is StarStage?") + paragraph (show format: auditions, semi-finals, grand finale, audience voting, prize details) + counter-up ("$10,000 Grand Prize"). Right: image (past winner celebrating on stage).

6. **columnsgrid (Performer Categories — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading (category: "Vocal", "Dance", "Comedy", "Specialty Acts") + caption (number of acts) + paragraph (what judges look for).

7. **tabs (Meet the Performers)**
   Tabs by category: Singers, Dancers, Comedians, Specialty. Each tab: columnsgrid (3 columns) with image (performer photo) + heading (name) + caption (act description) + paragraph (background and fun fact) + button ("Watch Audition Clip").

8. **layout-row (The Judges)**
   heading ("Your Judges") + columnsgrid (3 columns): image (judge headshot) + heading (name) + caption (credentials: "Grammy-winning producer", "Choreographer to the stars", "Comedy legend") + paragraph (brief bio).

9. **layout-row (Show Format)**
   heading ("How It Works") + columnsgrid (4 columns): icon + heading + caption. "Open Auditions", "Semi-Finals (Audience Vote)", "Judges' Wildcard", "Grand Finale & Winner".

10. **carousel (Past Show Highlights)**
   Photos and clips from previous seasons: performances, audience reactions, winner announcements.

11. **columnsgrid (Ticket Options — 3 columns)**
    Each: heading ("Semi-Final Night", "Grand Finale", "Season Pass (All Shows)") + paragraph (what is included) + counter-up (price) + badge (on Grand Finale: "Almost Sold Out") + button ("Buy Tickets").

12. **rating (Audience Favorites)**
    heading ("Last Season's Top Rated Acts") + columnsgrid (3 columns): image + heading (performer name) + rating (audience score) + caption (act type).

13. **form (Audition Application)**
    textbox (performer/group name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (act category), textbox (act duration), textarea (describe your act), checkbox (agree to terms), button ("Submit Application").

14. **ticker (Media & Sponsors)**
    Scrolling logos of broadcasting partners, sponsors, and media coverage outlets.

15. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Venue address, show dates, box office phone, email, social media (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok), audition FAQ link, press/media contact.

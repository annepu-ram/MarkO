# Film Festival / Screening / Cinema Event

> Focus: Film program discovery, ticket sales, schedule navigation, and filmmaker community engagement

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Sundance Film Festival | sundance.org | Program grid, film detail pages, schedule builder, pass tiers |
| Toronto International Film Festival | tiff.net | Film browsing by genre/country, screening times, industry events |
| Cannes Film Festival | festival-cannes.com | Cinematic hero imagery, official selection showcase, jury spotlight |
| Mumbai Film Festival (MAMI) | mumbaifilmfestival.com | Category tabs, venue map, filmmaker bios, volunteer registration |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Film program browsable by genre, language, or section (Competition, Showcase, Shorts)
- Schedule grid with screening times and venue info
- Filmmaker/director spotlights build prestige and interest
- Pass/ticket tiers (Single Screening, Day Pass, Festival Pass, Industry)
- Countdown to opening night creates anticipation
- Jury/selection committee profiles add credibility
- Submission portal for filmmakers (call for entries)

## Variant A — International Film Festival

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Festival logo, nav (Program, Schedule, Tickets, Filmmakers, Venues, About), button ("Buy Passes").

2. **video-background (Hero)**
   Montage of past festival highlights (red carpet, screenings, Q&A sessions, audience reactions), overlay: eyebrow ("October 8-15, 2026") + heading ("Horizon International Film Festival") + paragraph ("7 days. 120 films. 40 countries. One city.") + button ("Explore Program") + button ("Get Passes").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Festival Countdown)**
   heading ("Opening Night In") + countdown timer + caption ("Opening Film: 'Echoes of Tomorrow' by Priya Sharma").

5. **counter-up (Festival Stats)**
   "120 Films", "40 Countries", "8 Venues", "50+ World Premieres", "200+ Filmmakers Attending".

6. **tabs (Official Program)**
   Tabs: Competition, World Cinema, Documentaries, Short Films, Retrospective, Midnight Screenings. Each: columnsgrid (3 columns) with image (film still/poster) + heading (film title) + caption (director, country, runtime) + badge ("World Premiere" or "Award Winner") + paragraph (one-line synopsis) + button ("View Details").

7. **layout-row (Opening & Closing Films)**
   Left: image (opening film poster) + heading (film title) + caption (director) + paragraph (synopsis) + badge ("Opening Film") + button ("Book Seats"). Right: image (closing film poster) + heading + caption + paragraph + badge ("Closing Film") + button ("Book Seats").

8. **columnsgrid (Pass Tiers -- 4 columns)**
   Each: heading (pass name) + paragraph (inclusions) + counter-up (price) + badge (on Full Festival: "Best Value") + button ("Buy"). Tiers: Single Screening ($15), Day Pass ($45), Full Festival Pass ($180), Industry Pass ($350 -- includes market, networking events, filmmaker lounge).

9. **layout-row (Filmmaker Spotlight)**
   heading ("Featured Filmmakers") + columnsgrid (4 columns): each with image (headshot) + heading (name) + caption (country, film title) + paragraph (brief bio) + button ("View Filmography").

10. **carousel (Past Festival Highlights)**
   Photos: red carpet arrivals, packed screening halls, Q&A panels, award ceremony moments, audience reactions.

11. **layout-row (Jury)**
    heading ("2026 Jury Panel") + columnsgrid (3 columns): each with image (jury member) + heading (name) + caption (title -- "Award-Winning Director", "Film Critic", "Actor") + paragraph (notable works).

12. **accordion (Venues & Schedule Info)**
    One section per venue: heading (venue name), paragraph (address, capacity, accessibility info, nearby parking), image (venue photo).

13. **form (Newsletter & Filmmaker Submissions)**
    heading ("Stay Updated / Submit Your Film") + textbox (name), textbox (email), dropdown (interest: Attend as Audience / Submit a Film / Press Accreditation / Volunteer), textarea (message or film details), button ("Submit").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    columnsgrid: Column 1 -- festival logo + paragraph (about). Column 2 -- links (Program, Schedule, Tickets, Venues). Column 3 -- links (Submit Film, Press, Volunteer, Partners). Column 4 -- icon (social links) + caption (press email) + caption (info email).

## Variant B — Short Film / Indie Film Festival

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Festival name, nav (Films, Submit, Tickets, Workshops, About), button ("Submit Your Film").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: eyebrow ("Celebrating Independent Cinema") + heading ("IndieLens Short Film Festival 2026") + paragraph ("A platform for bold, original voices. 80 short films. 3 days. One unforgettable celebration of indie filmmaking.") + button ("View Program") + button ("Buy Tickets"). Right: image (dramatic still from a short film or indie cinema aesthetic -- projector light, film reel).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Submission Deadline)**
   heading ("Submissions Close In") + countdown timer + caption ("Early bird fee: $20 | Regular: $35") + button ("Submit Now").

5. **counter-up (Festival Numbers)**
   "80 Short Films", "25 Countries", "3 Screening Days", "1,200+ Submissions Received", "12 Award Categories".

6. **tabs (Film Program)**
   Tabs: Narrative Shorts, Documentary Shorts, Animation, Experimental, Student Films, Music Videos. Each: columnsgrid (2 columns) with image (film still) + heading (title) + caption (director, country, duration) + paragraph (logline) + badge ("Official Selection") + button ("Add to Watchlist").

7. **layout-row (Submission Guidelines)**
   Left: heading ("Call for Entries") + paragraph ("We welcome short films under 30 minutes in all genres. First-time filmmakers encouraged.") + accordion: "Eligibility Criteria", "Submission Fees", "Required Materials", "Selection Process", "Important Dates". Right: image (filmmaker behind camera).

8. **columnsgrid (Ticket Options -- 3 columns)**
   Each: heading + paragraph (what's included) + counter-up (price) + button. Single Screening ($10), Day Pass ($25), Festival Pass ($60 -- all screenings + workshops + closing party).

9. **layout-row (Workshops & Panels)**
   heading ("Learn from the Best") + columnsgrid (3 columns): each with icon + heading (workshop title) + caption (speaker name) + paragraph (description) + badge ("Free with Festival Pass"). Topics: "Screenwriting for Shorts", "Micro-Budget Filmmaking", "Getting into Film Festivals".

10. **carousel (Award Winners -- Past Years)**
   Each slide: image (winning film still) + heading (film title) + caption (director, year, award category) + paragraph (jury citation).

11. **ticker (Laurels / Partners)**
    Scrolling partner logos: film commissions, sponsors, media partners, cultural organizations.

12. **layout-row (Filmmaker Testimonials)**
    heading ("Filmmaker Voices") + columnsgrid (2 columns): each with blockquote + caption (filmmaker name, film title, year) + rating.

13. **form (Submit Your Film)**
    textbox (film title), textbox (director name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (category: Narrative / Documentary / Animation / Experimental / Student / Music Video), textbox (runtime in minutes), textbox (film link -- Vimeo/YouTube private link), textarea (synopsis), textarea (director's statement), button ("Submit Entry").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    columnsgrid: Column 1 -- festival logo + paragraph (mission statement). Column 2 -- links (Program, Submit, Tickets, Workshops, FAQ). Column 3 -- links (Press Kit, Volunteer, Sponsorship, Contact). Column 4 -- icon (social links) + caption (email).

## Variant C — Film Premiere / Red Carpet Event

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Film title / production house logo, nav (The Film, Cast & Crew, Premiere Night, Gallery, Tickets), button ("Get Premiere Tickets").

2. **video-background (Hero)**
   Official film trailer playing as background, overlay: eyebrow ("World Premiere -- December 12, 2026") + heading (film title in large cinematic typography) + paragraph ("A story that will stay with you long after the credits roll.") + button ("Watch Trailer") + button ("Book Premiere Tickets").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **layout-row (About the Film)**
   Left: image (official movie poster). Right: heading (film title) + eyebrow (genre + rating + runtime) + paragraph (synopsis -- 3-4 sentences) + caption ("Directed by [Name] | Produced by [Studio]") + badge ("Official Selection -- Cannes 2026").

5. **layout-row (Cast & Crew Spotlight)**
   heading ("Meet the Cast") + columnsgrid (4 columns): each with image (headshot) + heading (actor name) + caption (character name / role) + paragraph (notable previous works).

6. **layout-row (Director's Vision)**
   Left: heading ("Director's Note") + blockquote ("This film is a love letter to resilience. Every frame carries the weight of stories I grew up hearing.") + caption (director name). Right: image (behind-the-scenes photo of director on set).

7. **columnsgrid (Premiere Night Details -- 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph. "Red Carpet" (6:00 PM -- arrivals, media, photo ops), "Screening" (7:30 PM -- exclusive first viewing, Dolby Atmos), "After Party" (10:00 PM -- cocktails, live music, cast meet-and-greet).

8. **columnsgrid (Ticket Tiers -- 3 columns)**
   Each: heading + paragraph (inclusions) + counter-up (price) + badge (on VIP: "Limited Availability") + button ("Purchase"). Standard ($50 -- screening only), Premium ($120 -- screening + after party + signed poster), VIP ($250 -- red carpet access + screening + reserved seating + after party + photo with cast).

9. **countdown (Premiere Countdown)**
   heading ("Premiere Night In") + countdown timer + caption ("IMAX Grand Theatre, Mumbai").

10. **carousel (Behind the Scenes)**
   BTS photos and stills: on-set moments, rehearsals, location scouting, post-production, promotional shoots.

11. **layout-row (Reviews & Early Reactions)**
    heading ("What Critics Are Saying") + columnsgrid (3 columns): each with rating + blockquote (review excerpt) + caption (critic name, publication) + badge ("Must Watch").

12. **layout-row (Venue & Logistics)**
    Left: heading ("Venue") + paragraph (IMAX Grand Theatre address, parking info, dress code -- formal/black tie) + icon + caption ("Doors open at 5:30 PM") + icon + caption ("Free valet parking"). Right: image (venue exterior).

13. **form (Ticket Purchase / RSVP)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (ticket tier: Standard / Premium / VIP), textbox (number of tickets), dropdown (how did you hear about the premiere), button ("Complete Purchase").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    columnsgrid: Column 1 -- film logo/poster thumbnail + caption (release date). Column 2 -- links (The Film, Cast & Crew, Premiere, Press Kit). Column 3 -- links (Production House, Distribution, Contact). Column 4 -- icon (social links) + caption (PR contact email).

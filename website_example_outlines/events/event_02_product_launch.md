# Product Launch Events (Bikes, Cars, Phones, Gadgets)

> Focus: High-energy reveal events that build anticipation, showcase innovation, and drive pre-orders for new products

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Apple Events | apple.com/apple-events | Cinematic hero video, spec counter-ups, product carousel with angles |
| Tesla Cybertruck Reveal | tesla.com/events | Full-screen video reveal, bold spec numbers, minimal copy with dramatic imagery |
| Samsung Unpacked | samsung.com/global/galaxy/events/unpacked | Countdown to event, product comparison tabs, live stream embed |
| Royal Enfield Launches | royalenfield.com | Lifestyle imagery, ride-culture storytelling, spec badges |
| OnePlus Launch Events | oneplus.com/launch | Countdown timer, spec highlights with counter-ups, community hype ticker |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Countdown timers are essential for building pre-event hype and urgency
- Hero section with dramatic product imagery or video background sets the tone instantly
- Spec highlights using counter-up components make numbers feel impactful
- Comparison tabs let visitors contrast the new product against predecessors or competitors
- Carousel of product angles/colors gives a hands-on feel before the physical reveal
- Pre-order or RSVP forms with limited-edition badges drive conversion
- Ticker with early reviewer quotes or media mentions builds social proof

## Variant A — Automobile Launch (Car / Bike Reveal Event)

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Brand logo, nav (The Vehicle, Specs, Gallery, Event, Pre-Book, Contact), button ("Reserve Yours").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (dramatic shot of the car/bike under stage lights, partially veiled) + overlay: eyebrow ("World Premiere") + heading ("Introducing the All-New Vortex GT 2026") + paragraph ("Performance redefined. Design reimagined. Arriving this summer.") + button ("Watch the Reveal") + button ("Pre-Book Now").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Reveal Event)**
   Countdown timer to the live unveiling date. Heading: "The Reveal Begins In".

5. **layout-row (Design Philosophy)**
   Left: heading ("Born from the Track") + paragraph ("Every curve, every line, every surface has been sculpted in the wind tunnel. The Vortex GT is the most aerodynamic vehicle we have ever created.") + blockquote ("We didn't design a car. We designed a feeling." — Chief Design Officer). Right: image (sculpted side-profile shot highlighting body lines).

6. **counter-up (Key Specifications)**
   "450 BHP", "0-100 km/h in 3.2s", "1,200 kg Kerb Weight", "520 km Range".

7. **tabs (Explore the Vehicle)**
   Tabs: Exterior, Interior, Performance, Technology. Each tab: layout-row with image (relevant product photo) + heading (section title) + paragraph (detailed description) + badge (standout feature — "Adaptive Aero", "Haptic Steering", "AI Co-Pilot").

8. **carousel (360-Degree Gallery)**
   Slides: front three-quarter, rear profile, cockpit interior, instrument cluster close-up, wheel design, engine bay, the vehicle on a mountain road, studio shots in available colors.

9. **columnsgrid (Available Configurations — 3 columns)**
   Each: image (color swatch or trim shot) + heading (trim name — "Sport", "GT Pro", "Launch Edition") + paragraph (key inclusions) + counter-up (starting price) + badge ("Limited to 500 Units" on Launch Edition) + button ("Configure Yours").

10. **layout-row (The Launch Event)**
   Left: heading ("Be There for the Unveiling") + paragraph ("Join us at the Grand Arena on June 15, 2026 for the global premiere. Live music, test drive experiences, and the first-ever public viewing.") + icon (calendar) + caption ("June 15, 2026 — 7:00 PM") + icon (map pin) + caption ("Grand Arena, Mumbai"). Right: image (venue rendering or past launch event crowd).

11. **accordion (Event Schedule)**
    Panels: "5:30 PM — Gates Open & Welcome Lounge", "6:00 PM — Brand Film Screening", "7:00 PM — The Grand Reveal on Stage", "7:30 PM — Walk-Around & Static Display", "8:00 PM — Test Drive Bookings Open", "8:30 PM — Live Performance & After-Party".

12. **ticker (Media Reactions)**
    Scrolling cards: blockquote ("A genuine game-changer in the segment") + caption ("AutoCar India") | blockquote ("The design language is years ahead") + caption ("Top Gear") | more media quotes.

13. **form (Pre-Book / Register Interest)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), textbox (phone number), dropdown (preferred trim: "Sport", "GT Pro", "Launch Edition"), dropdown (city/dealership), checkbox ("I'd like a private preview appointment"), button ("Pre-Book Now").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Brand logo, dealership locator link, customer care number, social media links (Instagram, YouTube, Twitter/X, Facebook), link ("Press Kit & Media"), link ("Careers"), copyright and legal disclaimer.

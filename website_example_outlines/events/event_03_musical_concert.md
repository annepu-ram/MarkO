# Musical Events / Concerts

> Focus: Electrifying event pages that capture the energy of live music, drive ticket sales, and build artist-fan connection

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Coachella | coachella.com | Bold poster-style hero, lineup grid by day, countdown, ticket tiers |
| Tomorrowland | tomorrowland.com | Immersive video background hero, artist cards, stage schedule tabs |
| Sydney Opera House | sydneyoperahouse.com | Elegant event cards, season calendar, performer bios, accessible booking |
| Boiler Room | boilerroom.tv | Raw photography, artist profiles, event archive, livestream focus |
| Blue Note Jazz Club | bluenotejazz.com | Artist headshots, show schedule accordion, dinner-and-show packages, intimate venue feel |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Video background or dramatic imagery in the hero sets the sonic/visual mood instantly
- Countdown timers are standard for building pre-event anticipation
- Lineup or artist grid is the most-visited section — organize by day, stage, or genre using tabs
- Ticket tier comparison with badges for VIP or early-bird drives urgency
- Carousel of past event photos/videos gives a taste of the live experience
- Ticker with artist names or song snippets creates a sense of movement and energy
- Accordion for detailed set times and stage schedules keeps the page scannable

## Variant A — Live Concert / Music Festival

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Festival logo, nav (Lineup, Schedule, Tickets, Venue, Gallery, FAQ, Contact), button ("Get Tickets").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (massive crowd with stage lights, confetti, and a performer silhouette) + overlay: eyebrow ("October 18-20, 2026 — Meadow Grounds, Pune") + heading ("SonicWave Festival 2026") + paragraph ("Three days. Four stages. Fifty artists. One unforgettable weekend.") + button ("Buy Passes") + button ("See the Lineup").

3. **countdown (Festival Countdown)**
   Countdown timer to the festival opening day. Heading: "The Music Starts In".

4. **counter-up (Festival at a Glance)**
   "50+ Artists", "4 Stages", "3 Days", "25,000 Fans Expected".

5. **tabs (Lineup by Day)**
   Tabs: Day 1 — Friday, Day 2 — Saturday, Day 3 — Sunday. Each tab: columnsgrid (4 columns) with image (artist photo) + heading (artist name) + caption (genre — "Indie Rock", "Hip-Hop", "Electronic") + badge ("Headliner" on top acts) + caption (set time — "9:00 PM — Main Stage").

6. **accordion (Stage Schedules)**
   Panels per stage: "Main Stage — Thunder Arena" with paragraph (chronological set list with times and artist names), "Stage 2 — Bassline Tent" similarly, "Stage 3 — Acoustic Garden", "Stage 4 — DJ Dome".

7. **carousel (Past Festival Highlights)**
   Slides: crowd energy shots, close-ups of performers on stage, aerial drone view of the festival grounds, backstage candids, fans with festival merch, sunset over the venue.

8. **columnsgrid (Ticket Tiers — 3 columns)**
   Each: heading (tier name — "General Admission", "VIP Experience", "Backstage All-Access") + paragraph (inclusions — stage access, lounge, meet-and-greet, merch pack) + counter-up (price) + badge ("Early Bird — 20% Off" on General, "Selling Fast" on VIP) + button ("Buy Now").

9. **layout-row (Venue & Getting There)**
   Left: image (venue map or aerial photo of the festival grounds). Right: heading ("Meadow Grounds, Pune") + paragraph ("Located 30 minutes from Pune city center with dedicated shuttle buses, on-site parking, and camping zones.") + icon (map pin) + caption ("GPS: 18.5204 N, 73.8567 E") + icon (bus) + caption ("Free shuttles from Pune Station every 30 min") + button ("View Venue Map").

10. **layout-row (Sponsors & Partners)**
    ticker: Scrolling sponsor logos — beverage brands, audio equipment companies, streaming platforms, lifestyle brands.

11. **layout-row (Stay Connected)**
    Left: heading ("Don't Miss a Beat") + paragraph ("Get lineup announcements, set time updates, and exclusive pre-sale codes straight to your inbox."). Right: form with textbox (name), textbox (email), dropdown (interest: "Full Lineup Updates", "Ticket Alerts", "Camping Info"), checkbox ("I agree to receive festival communications"), button ("Sign Me Up").

12. **accordion (FAQ)**
    Panels: "What can I bring to the festival?", "Is there an age restriction?", "Can I re-enter the venue?", "What happens if it rains?", "Are there food and drink options on-site?", "Is camping available?".

13. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Festival logo, dates and venue address, organizer contact, social media (Instagram, YouTube, Spotify Playlist, Twitter/X, Facebook), link ("Press & Media"), link ("Terms & Conditions"), link ("Code of Conduct"), sponsor acknowledgment row, copyright.

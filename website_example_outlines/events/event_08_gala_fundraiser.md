# Gala / Charity Fundraiser / Awards Night

> Focus: Prestige-driven, donation-focused, elegant single-evening events

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Robin Hood Foundation | robinhood.org | Impact stats front-and-center, donor tiers, emotional storytelling |
| Met Gala (Vogue) | vogue.com/met-gala | Full-bleed cinematic imagery, celebrity social proof, countdown |
| St. Jude Gala | stjude.org | Mission-first messaging, progress bars for fundraising goals, donor recognition |
| amfAR | amfar.org | Auction highlights, past event galleries, sponsor logo ticker |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Countdown to event date creates urgency
- Fundraising progress bar shows momentum and encourages donations
- Donor/sponsor tier recognition encourages higher giving levels
- Emotional impact storytelling (beneficiary stories) drives engagement
- Auction item previews generate excitement
- Past event galleries establish credibility and glamour

## Variant A — Charity Gala / Black-Tie Fundraiser

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Charity name/logo, nav (About, The Gala, Auction, Sponsors, Impact, RSVP), button ("Donate Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width background image (elegantly decorated ballroom with candlelit tables), overlay: eyebrow ("Annual Black-Tie Fundraiser") + heading ("An Evening of Hope & Generosity") + paragraph ("Join us for a night of dining, entertainment, and giving back") + button ("Reserve Your Table") + button ("Make a Donation").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Event Countdown)**
   Countdown timer to gala date, heading ("The Gala Begins In..."), elegant styling.

5. **layout-row (Our Mission)**
   Left: image (beneficiaries, community impact). Right: heading ("Why We Gather") + paragraph (charity mission, what funds support) + counter-up ("$2.5M Raised Last Year") + button ("Learn More").

6. **progress-bar (Fundraising Goal)**
   heading ("2026 Fundraising Goal: $500,000") + progress-bar (showing current donations toward goal) + paragraph ("Every dollar funds meals, shelter, and education for families in need").

7. **columnsgrid (Table Packages — 3 columns)**
   Each: badge (tier name) + heading (package name: "Silver Table", "Gold Table", "Platinum Table") + paragraph (inclusions: seats, wine, recognition) + counter-up (price) + button ("Reserve"). Platinum column gets badge ("Most Popular").

8. **tabs (Auction Preview)**
   Tabs: Live Auction, Silent Auction, Raffle. Each tab: columnsgrid with image + heading (item name) + caption (description) + paragraph (estimated value).

9. **layout-row (Entertainment & Program)**
   heading ("An Unforgettable Evening") + columnsgrid (4 columns): icon + heading + caption. "Cocktail Reception", "Gourmet Dinner", "Live Entertainment", "Auction & Awards".

10. **carousel (Past Gala Highlights)**
   Photos from previous galas: decorated venues, guests in formal attire, emotional moments, award presentations.

11. **ticker (Sponsor Recognition)**
    Scrolling sponsor logos with heading ("Our Generous Sponsors").

12. **layout-row (Beneficiary Story)**
    Left: image (beneficiary portrait). Right: blockquote (personal testimonial from someone helped by the charity) + caption (name, story context) + button ("Read More Stories").

13. **form (RSVP / Reserve a Table)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (table package), textbox (number of guests), dropdown (dietary requirements), checkbox (add donation), textarea (special requests), button ("Complete Reservation").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Charity address, phone, email, social media links, tax-deductible donation info, privacy policy.

## Variant B — Awards Ceremony / Recognition Night

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Organization name, nav (Awards, Nominees, Schedule, Past Winners, Sponsors, Register), button ("Get Tickets").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Background image (grand stage with spotlight and podium), overlay: eyebrow ("12th Annual Industry Awards") + heading ("Celebrating Excellence in Innovation") + paragraph ("Honoring the leaders, creators, and visionaries shaping our industry") + button ("View Nominees") + button ("Get Tickets").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Ceremony Countdown)**
   heading ("The Awards Night Begins In...") + countdown timer.

5. **columnsgrid (Award Categories — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading (category name: "Innovator of the Year", "Rising Star", "Lifetime Achievement", "Community Impact") + caption (brief description) + button ("See Nominees").

6. **tabs (Nominees by Category)**
   Tab per award category. Each tab: columnsgrid (3 columns) with image (nominee headshot) + heading (name) + caption (organization) + paragraph (nomination reason) + badge ("Finalist").

7. **layout-row (Keynote Speaker)**
   Left: image (speaker portrait). Right: eyebrow ("Keynote Address") + heading (speaker name) + paragraph (bio, achievements) + caption (topic title).

8. **layout-row (Evening Program)**
   heading ("Schedule of Events") + columnsgrid (4 columns): icon + heading + caption. "Red Carpet & Cocktails 6:00 PM", "Dinner Service 7:00 PM", "Awards Presentation 8:00 PM", "After-Party 10:00 PM".

9. **carousel (Past Winners Gallery)**
   Photos of previous award winners receiving trophies, acceptance speeches, celebratory moments.

10. **counter-up (Event Stats)**
   "500+ Guests", "12 Award Categories", "50+ Nominees", "11 Years Running".

11. **columnsgrid (Ticket Tiers — 3 columns)**
    Each: heading (tier: "Individual", "Corporate Table", "VIP Experience") + paragraph (what is included) + counter-up (price) + badge (on VIP: "Limited Seats") + button ("Purchase").

12. **ticker (Past Sponsors & Partners)**
    Scrolling logos of sponsors and media partners.

13. **form (Ticket Registration)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (company), dropdown (ticket tier), textbox (number of tickets), dropdown (dietary preference), checkbox (receive updates), button ("Register Now").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Organization contact info, social media, media inquiry email, past ceremony archives link.

## Variant C — Auction Event / Benefit Dinner

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Event name, nav (About, Auction Items, Dinner, Sponsors, Donate, Register), button ("Place a Bid").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Background image (sophisticated dinner setting with auction signage), overlay: heading ("The Annual Benefit Auction & Dinner") + paragraph ("Fine dining, extraordinary auction items, and a cause worth supporting") + button ("Browse Auction") + button ("Reserve Your Seat").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Event Night Countdown)**
   heading ("Bidding Opens In...") + countdown timer to event date.

5. **layout-row (The Cause)**
   Left: heading ("Why Your Bid Matters") + paragraph (cause description, how auction proceeds are used) + counter-up ("$1.8M raised to date") + button ("Donate Directly"). Right: image (impact photo showing the cause in action).

6. **tabs (Auction Catalog)**
   Tabs: Art & Collectibles, Travel & Experiences, Fine Dining, Sports Memorabilia. Each tab: columnsgrid (3 columns) with image + heading (item name) + paragraph (description) + caption ("Estimated Value: $X,XXX") + button ("Preview Item").

7. **progress-bar (Fundraising Thermometer)**
   heading ("Fundraising Progress") + progress-bar (current total vs. goal) + paragraph ("Help us reach our $300,000 goal").

8. **layout-row (Dinner Experience)**
   Left: image (plated gourmet meal). Right: eyebrow ("Gourmet Benefit Dinner") + heading ("A Five-Course Culinary Journey") + paragraph (chef name, menu preview) + caption ("Paired wines included with every course").

9. **columnsgrid (Seating Options — 3 columns)**
   Each: heading ("Individual Seat", "Couple Package", "Table of 10") + paragraph (inclusions: dinner, drinks, auction paddle, program) + counter-up (price) + button ("Reserve").

10. **carousel (Previous Auction Highlights)**
   Photos of past events: guests bidding, auction items on display, winning moments, dinner ambiance.

11. **layout-row (Featured Auction Item)**
    Left: image (high-value item like artwork or vacation package). Right: heading (item name) + paragraph (detailed description, provenance) + caption ("Starting Bid: $5,000") + badge ("Top Lot") + button ("Pre-Register to Bid").

12. **ticker (Sponsor & Donor Recognition)**
    Scrolling logos and names of major sponsors and benefactors.

13. **form (Registration & Pre-Bidding)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (seating option), checkbox (register for live auction paddle), checkbox (register for silent auction), textarea (items of interest), button ("Complete Registration").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Event venue address, date and time, contact phone, email, social media, charitable registration number, tax receipt info.

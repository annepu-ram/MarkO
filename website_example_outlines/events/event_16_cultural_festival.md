# Cultural Festival / Heritage Event / Community Celebration

> Focus: Event schedule, cultural immersion storytelling, performer showcases, and community participation

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Navratri Raas | navratripass.com | Night-wise schedule, artist lineup, pass tiers, garba dress code |
| Edinburgh Festival Fringe | edfringe.com | Searchable program, venue map, performer profiles, ticket tiers |
| Jaipur Literature Festival | jaipurlitfest.org | Speaker lineup, session schedule, sponsor prominence, cultural imagery |
| Splendour in the Grass | splendourinthegrass.com | Artist cards, day-wise lineup, camping info, ticket countdown |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Day-wise or night-wise event schedule is essential for multi-day festivals
- Performer/artist lineup with photos and bios drives ticket sales
- Cultural imagery and rich visuals set the mood and tone
- Multiple pass tiers (single entry, full festival, VIP/backstage)
- Food, marketplace, and cultural activity listings beyond main performances
- Venue/logistics info (parking, dress code, what to bring)
- Countdown to opening creates urgency

## Variant A — Diwali / Navratri / Cultural Festival Night

1. **titlebar**
   Event logo (festive typography), nav (Schedule, Artists, Passes, Food & Market, Gallery, Contact), button ("Buy Passes").

Live music, celebrity performers, garba under the stars, and a food festival to remember.") + button ("Get Passes") + button ("View Lineup").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Festival Countdown)**
   heading ("Navratri Begins In") + countdown timer + caption ("Night 1: October 1, 2026 | GMDC Ground").

5. **counter-up (Festival Scale)**
   "9 Nights", "50,000+ Attendees Nightly", "15 Live Artists", "100+ Food Stalls", "Grand Dandiya Arena".

6. **tabs (Night-Wise Artist Lineup)**
   Tabs: Night 1, Night 2, Night 3... Night 9. Each: image (artist performing) + heading (artist/band name) + caption (genre -- "Bollywood Garba", "Fusion Dandiya", "Traditional Folk") + paragraph (brief about the artist, hit songs) + badge ("Celebrity Night" on select nights) + button ("Buy Pass for This Night").

7. **columnsgrid (Pass Tiers -- 4 columns)**
   Each: heading (pass type) + paragraph (inclusions) + counter-up (price) + badge (on Season Pass: "Save 40%") + button ("Buy Now"). Types: Single Night ($15), Weekend Pass ($35 -- Fri-Sun), Season Pass ($80 -- all 9 nights), VIP Lounge ($150 -- premium seating, backstage access, complimentary food).

8. **layout-row (The Experience)**
   heading ("More Than Just Garba") + columnsgrid (4 columns): each with icon + heading + paragraph. "Live Music Stage" (celebrity artists every night, DJ after midnight), "Food Festival" (100+ stalls -- street food, traditional sweets, fusion cuisine), "Cultural Market" (handmade jewelry, ethnic wear, handicrafts), "Kids Zone" (rides, face painting, puppet shows).

9. **carousel (Past Festival Gallery)**
   Photos: massive garba circle, decorated venue, food stalls, colorful traditional outfits, celebrity performers, light installations.

10. **layout-row (Dress Code & Tips)**
   Left: heading ("What to Wear") + paragraph ("Traditional chaniya choli or kurta pajama recommended. Comfortable shoes a must for dancing. No stilettos on the garba ground.") + paragraph ("Pro tip: Arrive early for parking. Carry a water bottle. Lockers available at Gate 3."). Right: image (group in festive traditional attire).

11. **layout-row (Venue & Logistics)**

"Venue" (GMDC Ground, Ahmedabad -- gates open 7:00 PM nightly), "Parking" (3,000-car lot, Gate B for VIP, shuttle from satellite parking), "Safety" (CCTV monitoring, medical aid stations, women's helpline, bag check at entry).

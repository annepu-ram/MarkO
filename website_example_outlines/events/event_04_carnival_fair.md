# Carnival / Fair / Funfair / Mela

> Focus: High Energy / Community Celebration / Family Entertainment

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Notting Hill Carnival | nhcarnival.org | Bold color palette, parade route maps, performer lineup grid |
| Sydney Royal Easter Show | eastershow.com.au | Attraction categories, ticketing tiers, daily schedule tabs |
| Pushkar Mela | pushkarcamelfair.com | Cultural storytelling, event calendar, photo-heavy galleries |
| Coney Island | coneyisland.com | Ride showcases, seasonal countdown, family-focused pricing |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Vibrant, high-energy imagery with bold colors and dynamic layouts
- Event schedule or daily program is essential for multi-day events
- Attraction/ride showcase cards with images help visitors plan
- Ticket pricing tiers with family/group bundles drive conversions
- Countdown timer builds anticipation before the event
- Food and entertainment previews increase excitement
- Safety info and accessibility details build trust for family audiences

## Variant A — City Carnival / Street Festival

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Carnival name/logo, nav (Schedule, Attractions, Performers, Food, Tickets, Contact), button ("Get Tickets").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width background image of colorful carnival parade, overlay: eyebrow ("August 15-18, 2026 | Downtown District") + heading ("Rio Street Carnival") + paragraph ("Four days of music, dance, parades, and non-stop celebration") + button ("Buy Passes") + button ("View Schedule").

3. **br (slant section breaker, vibrant accent color)**
   Angled slant divider transitioning from hero to countdown section.

4. **countdown (Event Countdown)**
   "The Carnival Begins In..." with days/hours/minutes/seconds countdown to opening day.

5. **counter-up (Festival Stats)**
   "200+ Performers", "50,000 Visitors Last Year", "4 Parade Routes", "80+ Food Stalls".

6. **tabs (Daily Schedule)**
   Tab per day (Day 1, Day 2, Day 3, Day 4). Each: heading (date + theme) + columnsgrid (3 cols): icon + heading (time slot) + caption (performance/activity name + location).

7. **columnsgrid (Attractions & Highlights — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + caption + badge. "Grand Parade", "Samba Stage", "Drum Circle", "Kids Zone", "Street Art Walk", "Night Glow Procession".

8. **layout-row (Featured Performers)**
   Left: heading ("Headliner Performers") + paragraph (lineup description). Right: carousel with performer images + caption (name + genre).

9. **columnsgrid (Food & Drink — 4 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + caption. "Brazilian BBQ", "Carnival Cocktails", "Street Churros", "Tropical Smoothies", "Jerk Chicken", "Acai Bowls", "Funnel Cakes", "Craft Beer Garden".

10. **layout-row (Ticket Pricing)**
   3 cards in columnsgrid: heading (tier) + paragraph (inclusions) + counter-up (price) + badge (on VIP: "Best Value") + button ("Buy Now"). Tiers: Day Pass, Weekend Pass, VIP All-Access.

11. **accordion (FAQ)**
    "What is the carnival route?", "Is there parking available?", "Are pets allowed?", "What about rain?", "Is it accessible for wheelchairs?", "Can I bring outside food?".

12. **layout-row (Volunteer & Participate)**
    Left: image (volunteers in action). Right: heading ("Join the Carnival Crew") + paragraph ("Be part of the magic — volunteer, perform, or host a stall") + button ("Apply to Participate").

13. **form (Contact & Group Bookings)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (enquiry type: General, Group Booking, Stall Vendor, Performer Application), textbox (group size), textarea (message), button ("Submit Enquiry").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address with map link, social media links (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok), sponsor logos in ticker, safety guidelines link, accessibility info.

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

   "The Mela Opens In..." countdown timer to event start.

12. **layout-row (Stall Booking)**
    Left: heading ("Book Your Stall") + paragraph ("Artisans, food vendors, and handicraft sellers — reserve your space") + button ("Apply for Stall"). Right: image (decorated stalls at the fair).

13. **form (Visitor Enquiry)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (interest: General Visit, Group Tour, School Trip, Artisan Participation, Food Vendor), calendar (planned visit date), textbox (group size), textarea (special requirements), button ("Submit").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Venue address with directions, helpline numbers, email, social media, government cultural body logo, accessibility and safety info.

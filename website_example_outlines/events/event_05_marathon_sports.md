# Marathon / Sports Tournament / Athletic Event

> Focus: High-energy competitive events showcasing athletic achievement, community fitness, and sporting excellence

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| TCS New York City Marathon | nyrr.org/tcsnycmarathon | Countdown to race day, course map imagery, runner registration flow |
| Berlin Marathon | bmw-berlin-marathon.com | Split hero with race stats, past winners carousel, sponsor ticker |
| IPL Cricket | iplt20.com | Team lineup grids, match schedule tabs, live stats counters |
| FIFA World Cup | fifa.com/worldcup | Tournament bracket accordion, venue showcase, ticket tiers |
| Ironman Triathlon | ironman.com | Athlete spotlight cards, multi-sport event tabs, training resources |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Countdown timers to race day or match kickoff are essential urgency builders
- Counter-up stats (finishers, countries represented, prize pool) create scale and excitement
- Course maps and venue imagery are hero-worthy visuals
- Schedule or fixture lists work best in tabs (by day) or accordion (by round)
- Sponsor logos in a ticker reinforce event credibility and scale
- Registration forms need clear category selection (5K, 10K, half, full) or team entry

## Variant A — City Marathon / Running Event

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Marathon logo, nav (About, Course, Register, Schedule, Results, Sponsors, Contact), button ("Register Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: large image (aerial shot of thousands of runners crossing a city bridge at sunrise). Right: eyebrow ("April 12, 2026") + heading ("Lakeside City Marathon 2026") + paragraph ("Join 15,000 runners on a stunning course through downtown, the waterfront, and historic neighborhoods") + button ("Register Today") + button ("View Course Map").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Race Day)**
   Countdown timer to race morning with heading: "Race Day In".

5. **counter-up (Marathon Stats)**
   "15,000 Runners", "42.2 km Course", "26 Countries", "8 Years Running".

6. **tabs (Race Categories)**
   Tabs: Full Marathon, Half Marathon, 10K Fun Run, Kids Dash. Each tab: paragraph (distance, start time, cutoff time, age requirements) + image (runners in that category from previous years) + badge ("Sold Out" on select categories or "New" on Kids Dash) + button ("Register").

7. **layout-row (Course Highlights)**
   Left: heading ("The Course") + paragraph (route description — start at City Hall, through Riverside Park, along the lakefront, finish at Olympic Plaza) + accordion with panels: "Start Zone & Wave Schedule", "Aid Stations (Every 3 km)", "Elevation Profile", "Road Closures & Spectator Zones". Right: image (illustrated course map or aerial route photo).

8. **columnsgrid (What's Included — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + caption. "Race Bib & Timing Chip", "Finisher Medal & T-Shirt", "On-Course Hydration & Nutrition", "Post-Race Festival & Food".

9. **carousel (Past Marathon Highlights)**
   Photos from previous years: mass start, elite runners breaking tape, emotional finishers, medal ceremonies, spectator crowds, charity runners in costume.

10. **layout-row (Race Day Schedule)**
   Left: heading ("Race Day Timeline") + accordion with panels: "4:30 AM — Expo & Bag Drop Opens", "5:30 AM — Elite & Wave 1 Start", "5:45 AM — Wave 2 Start", "6:00 AM — Wave 3 & Fun Run Start", "9:00 AM — Awards Ceremony", "10:00 AM — Post-Race Festival". Right: image (runners crossing the finish line arch).

11. **ticker (Sponsor Logos)**
    Scrolling sponsor logos: title sponsor, hydration partner, sports apparel, nutrition brand, media partner, city government seal.

12. **layout-row (Charity Partners)**
    Left: heading ("Run for a Cause") + paragraph (partner charities, fundraising incentives, guaranteed entries for charity runners) + counter-up ("$2.5M Raised Since 2018") + button ("Join a Charity Team"). Right: columnsgrid (3 columns) with image (charity logo) + caption (charity name).

13. **form (Registration)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), textbox (phone), dropdown (race category: Full Marathon, Half Marathon, 10K, Kids Dash), dropdown (t-shirt size: XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL), radio (gender category: Male, Female, Non-Binary), checkbox ("I accept the race waiver and terms"), textarea (medical conditions or special needs), button ("Complete Registration").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Marathon organizer name and logo, event date and city, contact email and phone, social media (Instagram, Facebook, Strava, YouTube), link ("Press & Media"), link ("Volunteer Sign-Up"), link ("Past Results"), copyright notice.

## Variant B — Cricket / Football Tournament

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Tournament logo and crest, nav (Teams, Fixtures, Venues, Tickets, Standings, News, Contact), button ("Buy Tickets").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (packed stadium under floodlights with players on the field) + overlay: eyebrow ("Season 4 — June 2026") + heading ("Premier City League 2026") + paragraph ("8 teams. 28 matches. One champion. The biggest cricket tournament returns to five iconic stadiums.") + button ("Match Schedule") + button ("Get Tickets").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Opening Match)**
   Countdown timer to the first match with heading: "First Ball In".

5. **counter-up (Tournament Scale)**
   "8 Teams", "28 Matches", "5 Stadiums", "200,000+ Expected Fans".

6. **tabs (Teams)**
   Tabs per team (or grouped): Team A, Team B, Team C, etc. Each tab: layout-row with image (team logo and squad photo) + heading (team name) + paragraph (team city, captain name, key players) + badge ("Defending Champions" on one team).

7. **columnsgrid (Match Fixtures — 3 columns)**
   Each card: heading (Team A vs Team B) + caption (date, time, venue) + image (venue thumbnail) + badge ("Night Match" or "Finals") + button ("Buy Tickets").

8. **accordion (Group Stage & Knockout)**
   Panels: "Group A — Fixtures & Standings", "Group B — Fixtures & Standings", "Semi-Finals", "Third Place Playoff", "Grand Final". Each panel: paragraph with match details, dates, venues, and results as they are announced.

9. **layout-row (Venues)**
   Left: heading ("Tournament Venues") + paragraph (five stadiums across the region, capacities, transport info). Right: carousel with slides per venue: image (stadium exterior or interior) + caption (stadium name, city, capacity).

10. **layout-row (Ticket Packages)**
   columnsgrid (3 columns): Each: heading ("Single Match", "Group Stage Pass", "All-Access Season Pass") + paragraph (what's included — seat category, food vouchers, merchandise) + counter-up (price) + badge ("Best Value" on Season Pass) + button ("Purchase").

11. **ticker (Sponsor & Broadcast Partners)**
    Scrolling logos: title sponsor, broadcast network, beverage partner, sportswear brand, banking partner.

12. **layout-row (Fan Zone)**
    Left: image (fan zone with screens, food stalls, activities). Right: heading ("The Fan Zone Experience") + paragraph (live screenings, meet-and-greet with legends, food court, kids activities, merchandise store) + button ("Fan Zone Info").

13. **form (Newsletter & Ticket Alerts)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), dropdown (favorite team: list of 8 teams), checkbox ("Send me match alerts and score updates"), checkbox ("I want early access to ticket sales"), button ("Subscribe").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Tournament organizer, governing body logo, broadcast schedule link, social media (Instagram, Twitter/X, YouTube, Facebook), link ("Accreditation & Media"), link ("Anti-Doping Policy"), link ("Terms & Conditions"), copyright.

## Variant C — Multi-Sport Championship

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Championship emblem, nav (Sports, Schedule, Athletes, Venues, Medals, Tickets, Volunteer), button ("Get Passes").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: heading ("National Sports Championship 2026") + eyebrow ("August 15-24 | Metro Sports Complex") + paragraph ("Over 2,000 athletes competing across 12 sports in the nation's premier multi-sport event") + button ("Full Schedule") + button ("Buy Tickets"). Right: image (collage or dynamic shot of multiple sports — track, swimming, gymnastics).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Opening Ceremony)**
   Countdown timer with heading: "Opening Ceremony In".

5. **counter-up (Championship at a Glance)**
   "12 Sports", "2,000+ Athletes", "150 Gold Medals", "10 Days of Competition".

6. **tabs (Sports Disciplines)**
   Tabs: Athletics, Swimming, Gymnastics, Basketball, Tennis, Cycling. Each tab: paragraph (events within the sport, venue, dates) + image (action shot of the sport) + columnsgrid (3 columns) of heading (event name) + caption (date, time) + badge ("Medal Event").

7. **layout-row (Venues & Facilities)**
   Left: heading ("World-Class Venues") + paragraph (purpose-built and renovated venues, accessibility information, transport links). Right: carousel with slides per venue: image + heading (venue name) + caption (sports hosted, capacity).

8. **accordion (Day-by-Day Schedule)**
   Panels for each competition day: "Day 1 — August 15 (Opening Ceremony + Prelims)", "Day 2 — August 16", through "Day 10 — August 24 (Finals & Closing Ceremony)". Each: paragraph listing sports in action, medal events, session times.

9. **columnsgrid (Athlete Spotlights — 3 columns)**
   Each: image (athlete portrait) + heading (athlete name) + caption (sport, event) + badge ("Defending Champion" or "Rising Star") + paragraph (one-line achievement summary).

10. **layout-row (Ticket Information)**
   columnsgrid (4 columns): Each: heading ("Session Pass", "Day Pass", "Sport Pass", "All-Sport Pass") + paragraph (what it covers) + counter-up (starting price) + button ("Buy Now"). Below: caption ("Children under 12 free with accompanying adult").

11. **layout-row (Medal Tally)**
    heading ("Medal Standings") + paragraph ("Updated live throughout the championship") + columnsgrid (3 columns): Each: image (region/team flag or crest) + heading (team name) + counter-up (total medals) + caption (gold, silver, bronze breakdown).

12. **ticker (Partner & Sponsor Logos)**
    Scrolling logos: government sports ministry, broadcast partners, sportswear official supplier, beverage sponsor, airline partner, technology partner.

13. **layout-row (Volunteer & Get Involved)**
    Left: image (volunteers in branded uniforms assisting at a venue). Right: heading ("Be Part of the Action") + paragraph (3,000 volunteers needed, roles available — event operations, athlete services, media, transport, medical) + button ("Apply to Volunteer") + caption ("Applications close June 30, 2026").

14. **form (Stay Updated)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), dropdown (primary interest: "Athletics", "Swimming", "Gymnastics", "Basketball", "Tennis", "Cycling", "All Sports"), checkbox ("Send me daily medal updates"), checkbox ("Notify me when tickets go on sale"), button ("Sign Up").

15. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Championship organizer and government body logos, event dates and city, contact details, social media (Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok), link ("Press Centre"), link ("Accessibility Info"), link ("Anti-Doping"), link ("Code of Conduct"), copyright.

# Summit / Conference / Symposium

> Focus: Thought leadership, speaker authority, and professional networking

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Web Summit | websummit.com | Bold countdown, speaker grid with filters, multi-track schedule |
| TED | ted.com | Speaker-centric layout, talk previews, community-driven feel |
| SXSW | sxsw.com | Multi-track tabs, badge tiers, sponsor ticker, past session videos |
| Davos / WEF | weforum.org | Data-rich impact stats, agenda by theme, partner logos, reports |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Speaker lineup is the primary draw — headshots, bios, and topics prominent
- Multi-track agenda organized by day or theme using tabs
- Badge/pass tiers with clear comparison of inclusions
- Countdown timer creates registration urgency
- Past event video or recap section builds credibility
- Networking or community features highlighted

## Variant A — Business Leadership Summit

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Summit name/logo, nav (Speakers, Agenda, Tickets, Sponsors, Venue, Contact), button ("Register Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Background image (packed auditorium with speaker on stage under dramatic lighting), overlay: eyebrow ("June 18-20, 2026 | Grand Convention Center") + heading ("Global Leadership Summit 2026") + paragraph ("Where visionary leaders converge to shape the future of business") + button ("Get Your Pass") + button ("View Speakers").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Summit Countdown)**
   heading ("Summit Begins In...") + countdown timer.

5. **counter-up (Summit Numbers)**
   "3,000+ Attendees", "80+ Speakers", "40+ Sessions", "25 Countries Represented".

6. **columnsgrid (Keynote Speakers — 4 columns)**
   Each: image (headshot) + heading (speaker name) + caption (title, company) + paragraph (talk topic) + button ("View Bio").

7. **tabs (Agenda by Day)**
   Tabs: Day 1 (Strategy), Day 2 (Innovation), Day 3 (Leadership). Each tab: columnsgrid with heading (session title) + caption (time, room) + paragraph (speaker name, description) + badge (track label like "Keynote" or "Workshop").

8. **layout-row (Why Attend)**
   Left: heading ("What You Will Gain") + paragraph (networking, insights, actionable strategies). Right: columnsgrid (2 columns) with icon + caption: "World-Class Speakers", "Hands-On Workshops", "Executive Networking", "Industry Reports".

9. **columnsgrid (Pass Tiers — 3 columns)**
   Each: heading ("Standard Pass", "Premium Pass", "Executive VIP") + paragraph (inclusions: sessions, meals, networking events, recordings) + counter-up (price) + button ("Purchase"). Premium gets badge ("Most Popular").

10. **carousel (Past Summit Highlights)**
   Photos from previous summits: packed sessions, networking events, panel discussions, exhibition areas.

11. **layout-row (Venue Information)**
    Left: image (convention center exterior). Right: heading ("Venue & Travel") + paragraph (address, nearby hotels, transport) + button ("Book Partner Hotel") + button ("Get Directions").

12. **ticker (Sponsors & Partners)**
    Scrolling logos of title sponsors, gold sponsors, and media partners.

13. **form (Registration)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), textbox (company), textbox (job title), dropdown (pass type), dropdown (dietary requirements), checkbox (opt-in to sponsor communications), button ("Complete Registration").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Organizer info, contact email, phone, social media, media/press contact, code of conduct link, refund policy.

## Variant B — Academic Conference / Research Symposium

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Conference name, nav (Call for Papers, Program, Speakers, Registration, Venue, Contact), button ("Submit Paper").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Background image (university auditorium with research presentation on screen), overlay: eyebrow ("15th International Symposium on Data Science") + heading ("Advancing Knowledge Through Collaboration") + paragraph ("Peer-reviewed research, keynote addresses, and scholarly exchange") + button ("Register") + button ("Call for Papers").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Submission Deadline)**
   heading ("Paper Submission Deadline In...") + countdown timer.

5. **layout-row (About the Conference)**
   Left: heading ("Conference Overview") + paragraph (scope, themes, academic significance, organizing body) + caption ("Indexed in Scopus and IEEE Xplore"). Right: image (researchers in discussion or poster session).

6. **tabs (Conference Tracks)**
   Tabs: Machine Learning, Data Engineering, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Ethics in AI. Each tab: heading (track description) + paragraph (topics of interest) + caption (track chair name and affiliation).

7. **columnsgrid (Keynote Speakers — 3 columns)**
   Each: image (headshot) + heading (professor/researcher name) + caption (university, department) + paragraph (research area and talk title) + button ("Read Abstract").

8. **layout-row (Important Dates)**
   heading ("Key Dates") + columnsgrid (4 columns): icon + heading + caption. "Paper Submission: Jan 15", "Notification: Mar 1", "Camera Ready: Apr 1", "Conference: Jun 18-20".

9. **accordion (Submission Guidelines)**
   Sections: "Paper Format & Length", "Review Process", "Presentation Guidelines", "Poster Session Requirements", "Publication & Indexing".

10. **columnsgrid (Registration Fees — 3 columns)**
   Each: heading ("Student", "Academic", "Industry") + paragraph (inclusions: proceedings, meals, social dinner) + counter-up (fee) + caption ("Early bird pricing until April 15") + button ("Register").

11. **layout-row (Venue & Accommodation)**
    Left: image (university campus). Right: heading ("Venue") + paragraph (university name, city, facilities) + button ("Campus Map") + button ("Accommodation Options").

12. **counter-up (Conference History)**
    "15 Editions", "4,500+ Papers Published", "60+ Countries", "200+ Universities Represented".

13. **ticker (Institutional Partners)**
    Scrolling logos of sponsoring universities, research councils, and industry partners.

14. **form (Registration / Paper Submission)**
    textbox (full name), textbox (email), textbox (affiliation), dropdown (registration type), dropdown (conference track), textbox (paper title), textarea (abstract), button ("Submit").

15. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Organizing committee contact, university address, conference email, social media, proceedings archive link, accessibility info.

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **countdown (Forum Countdown)**
   heading ("Forum Starts In...") + countdown timer.

5. **layout-row (Forum Overview)**
   Left: heading ("What Is the Forum?") + paragraph (purpose: bridging industry gaps, fostering dialogue, actionable outcomes) + button ("Download Agenda PDF"). Right: counter-up ("40+ Panelists") + counter-up ("12 Panels") + counter-up ("1,500 Attendees").

   Tabs: Future of Work, AI & Ethics, Sustainability, Digital Economy. Each tab: heading (panel title) + paragraph (discussion questions and scope) + columnsgrid (4 columns) with image (panelist headshot) + caption (name, role).

7. **columnsgrid (Featured Panelists — 4 columns)**
   Each: image (headshot) + heading (name) + caption (title, company) + paragraph (expertise and panel assignment).

8. **layout-row (Schedule)**
   heading ("Forum Schedule") + columnsgrid (3 columns): icon + heading + caption. "Registration & Coffee 8:00 AM", "Opening Remarks 9:00 AM", "Panel Block 1: 9:30-11:00", "Networking Lunch 12:00", "Panel Block 2: 1:30-3:00", "Fireside Chat 3:30", "Closing & Cocktails 5:00 PM".

10. **carousel (Past Forum Highlights)**
   Photos from previous forums: animated panel discussions, audience Q&A, networking moments.

11. **columnsgrid (Ticket Options — 3 columns)**
    Each: heading ("General Admission", "Professional Pass", "Corporate Table (6 seats)") + paragraph (inclusions: panels, lunch, recordings, networking lounge) + counter-up (price) + button ("Purchase"). Professional pass gets badge ("Includes Recordings").

12. **ticker (Forum Partners & Media)**
    Scrolling logos of industry association partners, media partners, and sponsors.

13. **form (Registration)**
    textbox (name), textbox (email), textbox (company), textbox (job title), dropdown (ticket type), dropdown (primary interest track), checkbox (interested in sponsorship opportunities), button ("Register").

14. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Organizer details, venue address, date, contact email, phone, social media, sponsorship deck download, past forum recordings link.

## Cross-Category Pattern Summary

### Universal Patterns (All Business Types)

- Sticky header/titlebar with primary CTA (Book, Shop, Get Quote, Enquire)
- Hero section is always the first major visual after navigation
- Trust signals (stats, testimonials, certifications, client logos)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Footer with multi-column links, contact info, social media

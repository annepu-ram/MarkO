# Real Website Research — Cross-Category Reference

> 150+ real websites analyzed across 30 business types (Feb 2026)
> See `_research_*.md` files for detailed per-site breakdowns.

---

## Universal Homepage Patterns (Found Across ALL Business Types)

| Pattern | Component | Frequency |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Sticky header with primary CTA | titlebar + button | 95%+ |
| Hero section (first visual after nav) | layout-row / carousel / video-background | 100% |
| Trust stats row | counter-up | 85%+ |
| Customer testimonials | carousel | 90%+ |
| Multi-column footer | layout-row + columnsgrid | 95%+ |
| Mobile-responsive design | All | 100% |
| Service/product card grid | columnsgrid | 90%+ |

## Business-Specific Patterns Discovered

### 1. Healthcare / Clinics
**Real Sites:** Apollo 24|7, Apollo Clinic, Clove Dental, Medanta
- **Search-first hero** with doctor/specialty search (Apollo 24|7)
- **Inline appointment form in hero** reduces friction (Clove Dental)
- **Before/After smile gallery** uniquely powerful for dental
- **Doctor profiles with ratings** build credibility
- **Emergency number in top bar** (Medanta double-row nav)
- **FAQ accordion** addresses patient objections on homepage
- **Health check packages** with transparent pricing
- **Clinic locator** critical for multi-location

### 2. Education / Coaching
**Real Sites:** Vedantu, Unacademy, Sri Chaitanya, BYJU'S
- **"Book Free Demo/Trial"** is the universal primary CTA
- **Live class schedule** creates urgency (Vedantu)
- **Topper faces with ranks** is the strongest trust signal
- **Grade/exam-based segmentation** organizes navigation
- **Video testimonials** from parents (BYJU'S)
- **Media coverage logo bar** builds credibility for ed-tech
- **Educator profiles with follower counts** (Unacademy social-media pattern)
- **Free resources** (sample papers, NCERT) drive top-of-funnel

### 3. Retail / Boutiques / Sweet Shops
**Real Sites:** Neerus, KALKI Fashion, Samyakk, Bombay Sweet Shop, Dadu's
- **Announcement ticker bar** for promos is standard e-commerce
- **Mega-menu navigation** for deep product catalogs
- **Instagram feed integration** drives social proof
- **Trust badges bar** near bottom (Free Shipping, Easy Returns, Secure Payment)
- **Celebrity/Influencer section** as social proof (KALKI)
- **Occasion-based navigation** for gifting businesses (Bombay Sweet Shop)
- **Festival-driven merchandising** with seasonal collections (Dadu's)
- **Showroom virtual tour** bridges online-offline (Samyakk)

### 4. Food & Beverage / Restaurants
**Real Sites:** Indian Accent, Bombay Canteen, Dhamaka NYC, HomeBakers, CakeNest
- **Minimal elegant design** with maximum photography for fine dining
- **Reservation is the single most prominent CTA** throughout
- **Chef spotlight** positions food as art (Indian Accent)
- **Instagram integration** keeps content fresh (Bombay Canteen)
- **Occasion-based categories** for cake/gifting businesses (CakeNest)
- **City-based filtering** critical for delivery (HomeBakers)
- **"How it Works" 3-step** reduces friction for platforms
- **Press/media quotes** are primary trust signal for premium restaurants

### 5. Events / Function Halls / Venues
**Real Sites:** Jade Banquets, Jaypee Hotels, WedMeGood, The Oberoi
- **Enquiry form is primary CTA** (not online booking — high-touch sales)
- **Venue capacity info** on cards helps visitors self-qualify
- **Virtual tour / 360° embed** reduces need for physical site visits (Jaypee)
- **Floor plans and capacity charts** are practical decision tools
- **Event type segmentation:** Weddings, Corporate, Social
- **Gallery is crucial** — visitors envision their event
- **Aspirational luxury-focused imagery** and language

### 6. B2B Manufacturing
**Real Sites:** UFlex, Fortran Steel, EPL, Fabrimech, SKV Engineering
- **"Get Quote / RFQ"** is the universal B2B CTA
- **Client logo bar** is the strongest B2B social proof
- **Global presence map** establishes international credibility
- **Certification grids** (ISO, ASME, BIS) are critical for manufacturing
- **Investor relations section** for publicly listed companies
- **Sustainability section** addresses modern buyer requirements
- **Industry sectors served** helps visitors self-identify
- **Drawing/specification upload** in contact forms

### 7. Home Services / HVAC / Pest Control
**Real Sites:** HiCare, PestQuit, TheSmartHVAC, Envigaurd, Aquachill
- **Free quote/inspection form embedded in hero** section
- **Floating call/WhatsApp buttons** on right edge
- **Service process steps** (Inspect → Treat → Protect → Monitor)
- **City-based service coverage** maps/grids
- **Comparison table** vs. competitors (TheSmartHVAC)
- **Price transparency** ("Starting from Rs. X")
- **Trust badges:** HACCP certified, government-approved chemicals

### 8. Professional Services / CA / Legal
**Real Sites:** G.K. Kedia & Co., Neeraj Bhagat, Trilegal, India Law Offices
- **Conservative navy/gold color palette** signals professionalism
- **Thought leadership content** (publications, insights, blog)
- **Partner/team profiles** with detailed credentials
- **Awards and directory rankings** (Chambers, Legal 500) prominently displayed
- **Client login portals** for ongoing service delivery
- **Tax calendar and calculators** as value-add tools
- **Legal disclaimers in footer** mandatory for law firms

### 9. Wholesale / Textile / Chemical
**Real Sites:** WholesaleTextile.in, Fabriclore, Pon Pure Chemicals, Trident

- **Brand directories** and partner logos
- **Catalog/brand-based browsing** alongside category browsing
- **Industry-application cross-referencing** for chemicals
- **B2B + D2C dual audience** (Fabriclore)

### 10. Photography Studios
**Real Sites:** Zero Gravity, Rajesh Digital, A. Rrajani, Portfolio Studio

## Top India-Specific Patterns

1. **WhatsApp as CTA** — floating button or link, especially Home Services, Wholesale, Food Delivery
2. **Regional language support** — Hindi/local language toggles
3. **Festival-driven sections** — Diwali, Dussehra, Holi seasonal collections/offers
4. **Gold rate tickers** — Jewellery stores
5. **GST/RERA/License numbers** — Footer legal compliance
6. **City-based filtering** — Critical for any location-dependent service
7. **"Since [year]"** heritage badges — Heritage is a major trust signal
8. **EMI/financing options** — For high-ticket items (dental, interior, jewellery)
9. **UPI/digital payment badges** — PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm logos
10. **FSSAI license display** — Mandatory for food businesses

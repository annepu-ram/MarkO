# Agriculture — Nurseries / Agri-Input Dealers

> Focus: Seasonal Demand

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| NurseryLive | https://nurserylive.com | Utility-based categorization (air purifying, stress relief), room-based categories, WhatsApp expert support, customer growth photos |
| PlantsGuru | https://plantsguru.com | Scale as trust (10M+ plants delivered), corporate gifting channel, mobile app for repeat customers, packaging quality in reviews |
| Greenways Nursery | https://greenwaysnursery.com | Physical nursery + landscaping services, "visit us" CTA, experience since 1992, service-based revenue |
| BigHaat | https://www.bighaat.com | AI crop diagnosis, real-time mandi prices, multi-language support, missed call feature, farmer community |
| FarmAtma | https://www.farmatma.in | Consultancy + e-commerce hybrid, DPR for bank loans as unique value, crop-specific navigation, expert team profiles |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Plant/crop categorization is primary navigation (by type, utility, or room placement)
- E-commerce patterns dominate for nurseries (product cards, add to cart, pricing)
- WhatsApp support is critical for Indian agricultural businesses
- Multi-language support is essential for farmer-facing platforms
- Trust signals: Delivery count stats, packaging quality reviews, establishment year
- Knowledge/care content drives SEO and reduces purchase anxiety
- Utility-based categorization (air purifying, stress relief) appeals to urban customers
- AI/tech features differentiate modern agritech (crop diagnosis, weather, mandi prices)
- Landscaping/consulting services as high-value add-on for nurseries
- Corporate gifting is additional revenue channel for plant nurseries

## Variant A — Plant Nursery / Garden Center

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Nursery name/logo, nav (Plants, Seeds, Garden Supplies, Landscaping, Contact), icon (leaf), button ("Shop Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero Split)**
   Left: heading ("Grow Your Green Paradise") + paragraph (wide variety, expert advice, delivery) + button ("Browse Plants"). Right: image (lush nursery / beautiful garden).

3. **ticker (Seasonal Highlights)**
   Scrolling: badge + caption — "Monsoon Sale", "New Succulents In", "Winter Flowers Available", "Organic Seeds".

4. **tabs (Shop by Category)**
   Tabs: Indoor Plants, Outdoor Plants, Flowering, Fruit Trees, Succulents & Cacti, Seeds, Herbs. Each: columnsgrid of plant cards — image + heading (name) + caption (price, pot size) + button.

5. **columnsgrid (Garden Supplies — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + caption. Pots & Planters, Soil & Compost, Fertilizers, Garden Tools, Pesticides, Irrigation.

6. **layout-row (Landscaping Services)**
   image (landscaped garden) + heading + paragraph (garden design, terrace gardens, maintenance) + button ("Get a Quote").

7. **layout-row (Expert Tips)**
   accordion: Seasonal planting guide, Watering tips, Pest management, Indoor plant care, Composting basics.

8. **counter-up (Nursery Stats)**
   "500+ Plant Varieties", "10,000+ Happy Gardeners", "Free Expert Advice", "Delivery Available".

9. **carousel (Customer Gardens)**
   Customer-submitted garden photos + caption.

10. **layout-row (Seasonal Calendar)**
    columnsgrid (4 cols) or tabs: Spring, Summer, Monsoon, Winter — recommended plants for each season.

11. **form (Order / Enquiry)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), dropdown (enquiry type: Plant Order / Landscaping / Bulk), textarea (details), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Nursery address, visiting hours, phone, WhatsApp, delivery areas, social links.

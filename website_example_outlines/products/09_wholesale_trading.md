## Variant A — Textile Wholesale / Fabric Distributor

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Company name/logo, nav (Products, Collections, About, Bulk Orders, Contact), button ("Request Price List").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (fabric rolls / textile warehouse), overlay: heading ("Premium Fabrics for Every Need") + paragraph (wholesale, competitive pricing, nationwide supply) + button ("Browse Collections").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **counter-up (Scale Indicators)**
   "500+ Fabric Varieties", "1,000+ Retail Partners", "Pan-India Delivery", "25+ Years in Business".

5. **tabs (Product Categories)**
   Tabs: Cotton, Silk, Polyester, Linen, Blended, Specialty Fabrics. Each: columnsgrid of fabric swatches — image + heading (fabric name) + caption (GSM, width, price/meter).

6. **columnsgrid (Industries We Supply — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + caption. Garment Manufacturers, Fashion Designers, Home Furnishing, Uniform Makers, Export Houses, Retail Stores.

7. **layout-row (Why Choose Us)**
   Left: heading + accordion items: Minimum Order Flexibility, Quality Assurance, Fast Dispatch, Custom Dyeing, Sample Service. Right: image (quality check process).

8. **layout-row (Bulk Pricing)**
   heading + paragraph ("Volume-based discounts") + columnsgrid (3 cols): heading (tier) + paragraph (quantity range) + badge ("Best Rate" on highest tier).

9. **ticker (Brand Partners / Mills)**
   Scrolling partner mill logos.

10. **carousel (Client Testimonials)**
   blockquote + caption (business name, city).

11. **layout-row (Logistics & Delivery)**
    icon + heading + paragraph (delivery network, packaging, tracking) + counter-up ("Dispatched within 48 hrs").

12. **form (Bulk Enquiry / Price Request)**
    textbox (company name), textbox (contact person), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (fabric type), textbox (quantity in meters), textarea (specifications — color, GSM, width), button.

13. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Registered office, warehouse address, GST number, phone, email, working hours.

## Variant B — Chemical Trading / Industrial Chemicals

**Page 1: Home**

1. **titlebar**
   Company logo, nav (Products, Industries, Safety, About, Contact), button ("Request MSDS / Quote").

2. **layout-row (Hero Split)**
   Left: heading ("Trusted Chemical Supply Partner") + paragraph (quality chemicals, safety-first approach, regulatory compliance) + button ("View Product Catalog"). Right: image (industrial drums / lab).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator between hero and content sections.

4. **columnsgrid (Product Categories — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + caption + link. Solvents, Acids & Alkalis, Specialty Chemicals, Lab Reagents, Industrial Cleaners, Adhesives & Resins.

5. **counter-up (Business Scale)**
   "200+ Chemical Products", "500+ Industrial Clients", "ISO 9001 Certified", "30+ Years".

6. **tabs (By Industry)**
   Tabs: Pharmaceuticals, Textiles, Food Processing, Construction, Automotive, Agriculture. Each: paragraph (chemicals supplied) + button ("View Products").

7. **layout-row (Safety & Compliance)**
   heading + columnsgrid (4 cols): icon + heading + caption. MSDS Available, Proper Labeling, Certified Storage, Trained Handling, Regulatory Compliance, Hazmat Transport.

8. **layout-row (Supply Chain)**
   3-step: icon + heading + paragraph. "Order Placement" → "Quality Testing & Packaging" → "Safe Delivery with Documentation".

9. **accordion (Technical Information)**
   Items: Storage guidelines, Handling precautions, Grade specifications, Purity certifications, Custom formulations.

10. **ticker (Certifications)**
   Scrolling certification badges: ISO, BIS, REACH, FDA.

11. **form (Quote / MSDS Request)**
    textbox (company), textbox (contact), textbox (email), dropdown (product category), textbox (chemical name), textbox (quantity), dropdown (grade/purity), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Office address, warehouse address, emergency contact, GST/CIN numbers, regulatory licenses.

**Page 2: Product Catalog**

1. **tabs (By Category)**
   Searchable product lists with CAS numbers, grades, packaging options.

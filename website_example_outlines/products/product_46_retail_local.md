# Local Retail — Hardware / Florist / Pet Store

> Focus: Local Trust & Product Discovery

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Ace Hardware | acehardware.com | Product categories grid, store locator, DIY tips, rewards program |
| The Bouqs Co | bouqs.com | Occasion-based navigation, same-day delivery, subscription options |
| Petco | petco.com | Pet type tabs, services + products, vet integration, loyalty program |
| Local hardware stores | various | Google Maps embed, hours prominently displayed, product category browse |
| FTD | ftd.com | Occasion-driven design, delivery date selector, price tiers |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Store hours and location prominently in hero or sticky bar
- Product category grids with icons and images
- Seasonal/occasion-based promotions
- Loyalty program or rewards callout
- DIY tips or care guides as value-add content
- Delivery/service area information

## Variant A — Hardware & Building Supplies Store

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Store name/logo, nav (Departments, Services, Brands, About, Contact), badge ("Open 7 Days"), button ("Store Hours & Directions").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: heading ("Your Neighborhood Hardware Store") + paragraph (tools, lumber, paint, plumbing — expert advice since 1995) + two buttons ("Shop Departments", "Get Directions"). Right: image (well-stocked store interior / helpful staff).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **columnsgrid (Departments — 4 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + caption. Power Tools, Plumbing, Electrical, Paint & Stain, Lumber & Building, Lawn & Garden, Hardware & Fasteners, Safety & Work Wear.

5. **layout-row (Why Shop Local)**
   columnsgrid (3 cols): icon + heading + paragraph. Expert Staff ("Ask us anything"), Special Orders ("If we don't have it, we'll get it"), Cut & Mix Services ("Lumber cutting, paint mixing").

6. **counter-up (Store Stats)**
   "30+ Years Serving the Community", "50,000+ Products", "Same-Day Pickup", "Free Project Advice".

7. **tabs (Services)**
   Tabs: Key Cutting, Paint Mixing, Lumber Cutting, Tool Rental, Delivery. Each: paragraph + pricing info + button.

8. **ticker (Brands We Carry)**
   Scrolling brand logos: DeWalt, Milwaukee, Behr, Weber, etc.

9. **layout-row (Store Info)**
   Left: heading ("Visit Us") + paragraph (address) + paragraph (hours for each day) + button ("Get Directions"). Right: image (storefront).

10. **carousel (Customer Reviews)**
    rating + blockquote + caption.

11. **form (Ask an Expert / Special Order)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (department), textarea (question or item needed), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, store hours, social links, delivery area info.

## Variant B — Florist / Flower Shop

**Page: Home (Single Page — Occasion-Driven)**

1. **titlebar**
   Shop name/logo, nav (Arrangements, Occasions, Delivery, About, Contact), badge ("Same-Day Delivery"), button ("Order Now").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Full-width image (stunning floral arrangement), overlay: heading ("Beautiful Blooms for Every Moment") + paragraph (handcrafted arrangements, same-day delivery) + two buttons ("Shop by Occasion", "Custom Arrangement").

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **columnsgrid (Shop by Occasion — 3 columns)**
   Each: image + heading + button. Birthday, Anniversary, Sympathy, Get Well, Thank You, Just Because.

5. **tabs (Our Collections)**
   Tabs: Signature Bouquets, Seasonal, Plants & Succulents, Wedding & Event. Each: columnsgrid of arrangement images + heading + caption (price range).

6. **layout-row (Custom Arrangements)**
   Left: image (florist at work). Right: heading ("Made Just for You") + paragraph (custom designs for weddings, events, corporate) + button ("Request Custom Quote").

7. **counter-up (Shop Stats)**
   "10,000+ Bouquets Delivered", "Same-Day Delivery", "100% Fresh Guarantee", "Locally Owned Since 2010".

8. **layout-row (Delivery Info)**
   heading + columnsgrid (3 cols): icon + heading + caption. Same-Day Delivery (order by 2pm), Nationwide Shipping, Subscription Plans.

9. **carousel (Happy Customers)**
   rating + blockquote + caption.

10. **form (Custom Order Request)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (occasion), calendar (delivery date), dropdown (budget range), textarea (preferences / flowers / colors), button.

11. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, shop hours, delivery areas, social links (Instagram).

## Variant C — Pet Store / Pet Supplies

**Page: Home (Single Page)**

1. **titlebar**
   Store name/logo, nav (Shop, Services, Pet Care, About, Contact), badge ("Locally Owned"), button ("Visit Us").

2. **layout-row (Hero)**
   Left: heading ("Everything Your Pet Needs, Under One Roof") + paragraph (premium food, toys, grooming, adoptions — family-owned since 2008) + two buttons ("Shop Products", "Grooming Appointments"). Right: image (happy pets / store interior).

3. **br (divider)**
   Visual separator.

4. **tabs (Shop by Pet)**
   Tabs: Dogs, Cats, Fish, Birds, Small Animals, Reptiles. Each: columnsgrid of product categories with icons + headings (Food, Toys, Beds, Health, Accessories).

5. **columnsgrid (Services — 3 columns)**
   Each: icon + heading + paragraph + button. Grooming, Nail Trimming, Adoption Events, Pet Nutrition Advice, Loyalty Rewards, Delivery.

6. **counter-up (Store Stats)**
   "15,000+ Products", "500+ Adoptions Facilitated", "15 Years Serving Pet Parents", "Free Nutrition Consultations".

7. **ticker (Brands We Carry)**
   Scrolling logos: Royal Canin, Blue Buffalo, Kong, Purina, etc.

8. **layout-row (Adoption Corner)**
   Left: heading ("Adopt, Don't Shop") + paragraph (partner shelters, adoption events every Saturday) + button ("See Available Pets"). Right: image (adoptable pet).

9. **carousel (Pet Parent Reviews)**
   rating + blockquote + caption (pet owner, pet type).

10. **layout-row (Store Info)**
    Left: heading + paragraph (address, hours). Right: image (storefront).

11. **form (Contact / Grooming Appointment)**
    textbox (name), textbox (phone), textbox (email), dropdown (service: General Inquiry, Grooming, Special Order), textbox (pet type & name), textarea (details), button.

12. **layout-row (Footer)**
    Address, phone, store hours, social links, delivery area.

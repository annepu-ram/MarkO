# Pharmacy & Medical — Medical Stores / Pharmacies

> Focus: Speed & Accuracy

## Real Website Examples & Key Insights

| Site | URL | Key Pattern |
|------|-----|-------------|
| PharmEasy | https://pharmeasy.in/ | Search bar dominant UI, prescription upload, 3-hour delivery promise, lab test cross-sell, subscription/auto-refill |
| Apollo Pharmacy | https://www.apollopharmacy.in/ | Healthcare ecosystem (pharmacy + doctors + lab + insurance), Circle membership loyalty tier, health records storage |
| Netmeds | https://www.netmeds.com/ | "By Health Issue" and "By Body Part" browsing, health library for SEO, beauty cross-sell, Refer & Earn |
| MedPlus Mart | https://www.medplusmart.com/ | Click & Pick (order online, pick at store), 3 ordering methods, health records, 4000+ physical stores O2O model |
| Wellness Forever | https://www.wellnessforever.com/ | 24/7 store availability, lifestyle products alongside medicines, 100,000+ product range, regional focus |

**Patterns to incorporate:**
- Search bar as primary UI element (medicine name search dominates)
- Prescription upload as primary ordering method (photo/PDF)
- Quick action icons (Order Medicine, Upload Rx, Lab Tests, Find Doctor)
- Health condition-based browsing (by disease or body part)
- Lab test packages as cross-sell alongside medicines
- Quality/trust signals (licensed pharmacy, genuine medicines, pharmacist verification)
- Multiple ordering methods (search, prescription upload, reorder from history)
- App promotion for repeat purchases
- Health content/blog for SEO and trust
- 24-hour delivery promise for speed competition

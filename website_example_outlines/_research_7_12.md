| Hero Carousel/Slider | Yes | Yes | Yes | Full-screen image | Yes | Yes |
| Service/Product Grid | Yes | Yes | Yes | Portfolio Grid | Yes | Program Grid |
| Counter Stats | Yes | Yes | Moderate | Yes | Moderate | Yes |
| Trust Badges/Certs | Yes | Yes | Yes | Awards | Awards | Brand heritage |
| Testimonials | Yes | Yes | Yes | Client stories | Before/After | Transformations |
| Contact Form/CTA | Prominent | Prominent | WhatsApp/Form | Minimal | Booking | "Join Now" |
| Location Finder | City coverage | Office list | Warehouse | Studio address | Store locator | Gym locator |
| Blog/Content | Tips | Thought leadership | Moderate | Stories | Beauty tips | Fitness tips |
| FAQ Accordion | Yes | Sometimes | Rare | Rare | Sometimes | Yes |
| Franchise Section | Rare | Rare | Rare | Rare | Yes | Yes |
| App Download | Sometimes | Rare | Sometimes | Rare | Rare | Yes |
| Pricing Display | Quote-based | Consultation | MOQ/Bulk | Packages | Treatment menu | Membership tiers |
| WhatsApp Float | Yes | Rare | Yes | Sometimes | Sometimes | Rare |

### Key Design Patterns per Business Type

1. **Home Services (HVAC / Pest Control):** Trust-heavy design with certifications, guarantees, and government approvals. Prominent phone numbers and toll-free lines. City-based service coverage maps. Free quote forms as primary conversion. Comparison tables vs. competitors. Process step visuals (Inspect > Treat > Protect). Floating call/WhatsApp buttons.

2. **Professional Services (CA / Legal):** Conservative, premium design with navy/gold palettes. Thought leadership content (publications, insights, blog). Partner/team profiles with credentials and photos. Practice area or service depth through structured mega-menus. Awards and directory rankings prominently displayed. Legal disclaimers in footer (mandatory for law firms). Client login portals for ongoing service delivery.

3. **Wholesale / Textile / Chemical Traders:** Catalog-heavy product grids with filtering and search. WhatsApp as primary communication channel. MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) displayed on products. Brand directories and partner logos. Price segmentation navigation ("Under Rs. 500"). B2B inquiry forms and bulk order CTAs. Industry-application cross-referencing for chemicals. Certification badges (ISO, quality standards).

4. **Photography Studios / Freelancers:** Image-first, minimal design where photos dominate. Dark/moody color palettes common for wedding photographers. Portfolio masonry grids as the centerpiece. Story-driven content (wedding stories, couple narratives). Video showreels for cinematography services. Instagram feed integration. Personal brand focus (photographer's name IS the brand). Tiered pricing packages (Silver/Gold/Platinum).

5. **Beauty & Wellness / Salons / Spas:** Appointment booking as the primary CTA throughout. Before/after transformation galleries for social proof. Serene, calming aesthetics for spas (earth tones, minimal). High-fashion glamorous aesthetics for salons. Store/salon locator critical for chains. Franchise recruitment as secondary business. Membership and gift card programs. Treatment menus with transparent pricing.

6. **Fitness / Gyms / Yoga Studios:** High-energy, bold visual design (black, neon, vibrant colors). "Join Now" CTA repeated throughout page. Gym/center locator as critical functionality. Before/after transformation stories as primary social proof. Membership tier comparison (Free, Basic, Premium). Mobile app promotion with QR codes and store badges. Franchise opportunity sections. Class schedules and program grids. Counter stats (members, locations, trainers, years).

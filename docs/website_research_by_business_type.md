Research of real website examples across 6 business categories, documenting homepage structure, navigation, sections, components, and notable patterns.

## 1. Healthcare / Small Clinics

5. **Stats/trust banner** -- numbers: 5000+ doctors, 100+ specialties, 15-minute consultations
6. **Doctor profiles carousel** -- featured doctor cards with photo, name, specialty, rating, and "Consult Now" CTA
7. **Health packages section** -- card grid of lab test packages with pricing
8. **App download banner** -- split-screen section with phone mockup and download links (App Store, Google Play)

**Notable Patterns:**
- Search-first hero pattern ideal for healthcare platforms
- Trust indicators (doctor count, specialties, response time) prominently displayed
- Quick-access service cards above the fold for key user journeys
- Rating and review integration in doctor cards builds credibility

- Sticky header/titlebar with primary CTA (Book, Shop, Get Quote, Enquire)

- Trust signals (stats, testimonials, certifications, client logos)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Footer with multi-column links, contact info, social media

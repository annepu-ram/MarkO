## Cross-Category Pattern Summary

### Common Patterns by Business Type:

| Pattern | Home Services | Professional Services | Wholesale/Trade | Photography | Beauty/Wellness | Fitness |
|---------|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Sticky Header | Yes | Yes | Yes | Sometimes | Yes | Yes |
| Hero Carousel/Slider | Yes | Yes | Yes | Full-screen image | Yes | Yes |
| Service/Product Grid | Yes | Yes | Yes | Portfolio Grid | Yes | Program Grid |
| Counter Stats | Yes | Yes | Moderate | Yes | Moderate | Yes |
| Trust Badges/Certs | Yes | Yes | Yes | Awards | Awards | Brand heritage |
| Testimonials | Yes | Yes | Yes | Client stories | Before/After | Transformations |
| Contact Form/CTA | Prominent | Prominent | WhatsApp/Form | Minimal | Booking | "Join Now" |
| Location Finder | City coverage | Office list | Warehouse | Studio address | Store locator | Gym locator |
| Blog/Content | Tips | Thought leadership | Moderate | Stories | Beauty tips | Fitness tips |
| FAQ Accordion | Yes | Sometimes | Rare | Rare | Sometimes | Yes |
| Franchise Section | Rare | Rare | Rare | Rare | Yes | Yes |
| App Download | Sometimes | Rare | Sometimes | Rare | Rare | Yes |

### Key Design Patterns per Business Type:

1. **Home Services:** Trust-heavy (certifications, guarantees), prominent phone numbers, city coverage, free quote forms
2. **Professional Services:** Conservative/premium design, thought leadership content, partner profiles, publication archives
3. **Wholesale/Trade:** Catalog-heavy, WhatsApp integration, MOQ display, B2B focus, brand directories
4. **Photography:** Image-first minimal design, portfolio masonry, dark/moody aesthetics, story-driven
5. **Beauty/Wellness:** Appointment booking, before/after galleries, serene aesthetics, franchise promotion
6. **Fitness:** High-energy bold design, gym locator, transformation stories, app promotion, membership tiers

Content Library & Campaign System

The Content Library is the Marketing CMS brain. It stores brand, product, offer, proof, media, and AI-generated content as reusable assets. Campaigns use this memory to create landing pages first, then future ads, email, social, and ecommerce outputs.

North Star
A Marketing Engine Powered by Content Memory
Content is not disposable page text. It is the reusable memory that lets AI create better campaigns every time.
1
Marketing CMS
Stores approved brands, products, offers, proof, copy, forms, and media.
2
AI Content Engine
Generates, rewrites, scores, and repurposes content from real context.
3
Campaign Studio
Combines goal, audience, offer, content, and page structure into a launchable campaign.
4
Launch Outputs
Creates landing pages now, then future email, ads, social, and ecommerce pages.
5
Learning Loop
Tracks winners, archives weak content, and improves future AI suggestions.

   - Sports: Action imagery + enrollment CTA
   - Logistics: Quote form inline with hero

   - Sports: Coach credentials, years of operation, student achievements
   - Logistics: Move count, tracking, insurance
   - Cleaning: Background verification, before/after photos
   - Jewellery: BIS hallmark, purity guarantee, exchange program
   - Pharmacy: Licensed pharmacist, quality checks, genuine medicines
   - Coworking: Client logos, security features, number of locations

3. **How It Works** -- 3-step process visualization is nearly universal

4. **Testimonials/Reviews** -- Every category relies on social proof

5. **Mobile app promotion** -- Especially strong for pharmacy, cleaning, logistics, coworking
